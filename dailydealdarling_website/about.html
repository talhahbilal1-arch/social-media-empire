About Daily Deal Darling

Our Mission

At Daily Deal Darling, we believe everyone deserves access to quality products without breaking the bank. We curate the best online deals and product recommendations so you can shop smarter, save more, and discover products you'll genuinely love. Our mission is simple: make shopping less overwhelming and more rewarding.

Our Editorial Process

DailyDealDarling curates daily product deals and affiliate recommendations through a rigorous editorial process. Our team manually reviews each deal for relevance, value, and genuine customer satisfaction. We don't use bots or auto-feeds — every recommendation goes through human review to ensure it meets our quality standards.

When selecting products to feature:

  • We prioritize products with 1,000+ verified reviews and 4.5+ star ratings on Amazon
  • We test many items personally and read detailed customer reviews to identify strengths and pain points
  • We avoid promoting products with high complaint rates, even if they have traffic potential
  • We compare current prices against historical highs to verify genuine deals (20%+ discount minimum)
  • We focus on honest, in-depth reviews rather than generic product lists

Affiliate Disclosure & Transparency

DailyDealDarling is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program and other affiliate programs. When you purchase through our links, we may earn a small commission — at no additional cost to you. This supports our work of testing, reviewing, and recommending products.

Here's what's important to know about our affiliate relationships:

  • Affiliate commissions never influence our editorial judgement or product recommendations
  • We disclose affiliate relationships transparently on all product pages
  • We only recommend products we genuinely believe provide value
  • Your purchase price is identical whether you use our links or shop directly — the affiliate relationship doesn't cost you anything

Not a Licensed Financial or Purchasing Advisor

Daily Deal Darling provides product recommendations and deal information for educational and informational purposes only. We are not licensed financial advisors, product safety experts, or purchasing consultants. Always verify product details, pricing, and availability on the retailer's website before making a purchase. Prices and availability change frequently, and we make no guarantees about current accuracy.
