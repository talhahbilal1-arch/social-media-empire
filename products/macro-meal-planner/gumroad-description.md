**Product Description (paste into Gumroad):**

Most meal plans fail because they tell you exactly what to eat, exactly when to eat it, and exactly how much. Then you open your fridge on Tuesday and realize you do not have tilapia, and the entire plan falls apart.

This planner works differently. Instead of prescribing meals, it gives you **templates**: mix-and-match building blocks where you choose the ingredients. Every template has a macro profile. You rotate templates through the week based on what you actually want to eat.

**What is inside:**

- 4 breakfast templates with per-serving macro breakdowns (scale to 40g, 60g, or 80g protein hits)
- 6 lunch templates covering bowls, wraps, salads, sandwiches, and leftovers
- 6 dinner templates covering beef, chicken, fish, vegetarian, one-pan, and slow-cooker meals
- 4 snack templates with calorie and macro profiles
- Weekly grocery list generator (pick your templates, extract the list, shop once)
- 2-hour Sunday meal prep workflow (shop Saturday, prep Sunday, eat all week)
- Eating-out survival guide for 8 restaurant types (Italian, Mexican, burger, pizza, Asian, steakhouse, fast-casual, brunch)
- The cheat meal rules (why the word "cheat" is the problem, and what to do instead)
- Fillable macro tracking spreadsheet spec (Google Sheets / Excel compatible)

**Who this is for:**

Men over 35 who train 3+ days per week, know they need to eat more protein, and want a system that works with their schedule instead of against it. Whether you are following the FitOver35 Body Recomposition Blueprint or any other training program, this planner handles the nutrition side.

**Who this is NOT for:**

Anyone looking for a rigid 7-day meal plan with exact recipes and shopping lists for each day. This is a template system. You choose the ingredients. The templates guarantee your macros land in the right range.
