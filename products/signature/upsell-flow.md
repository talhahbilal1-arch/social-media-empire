# Post-Purchase Upsell Flow

Everything below assumes the buyer just hit "Confirm purchase" on the $97 Blueprint.

---

## 1. Order Bump (Checkout page, pre-confirmation)

**Offer:** 90-Day Macro Meal Planner — add for $17.

**What it is:**
- 90 days of pre-calculated meals (breakfast, lunch, dinner, two snacks)
- Three calorie tiers: 2200 / 2500 / 2800 kcal, each macro-matched to Blueprint cut and grow phases
- Printable weekly shopping lists aggregated by store section
- Swap-in substitution table (200 interchangeable foods at equivalent macros)
- Delivered as a PDF plus editable Google Sheet

**Positioning line on checkout:**
"The Blueprint tells you what to eat. This tells you what to cook for 90 days straight. Add the Macro Meal Planner for $17 — normally $29."

**Take-rate target:** 25-35% of Blueprint buyers. At 100 Blueprint sales/mo, that is $425-595/mo incremental.

---

## 2. Day 3 Kit Email — Cross-Sell AI Fitness Vault

**Trigger:** 72 hours after Blueprint purchase. Kit automation tag `blueprint-buyer`.

**Subject:** "The one tool I use when the Blueprint feels like too much thinking"

**Angle:** The Blueprint is the program. The AI Fitness Vault is the assistant that runs alongside it — 150+ battle-tested ChatGPT prompts for substitutions, meal swaps, travel workouts, and plateau diagnostics.

**Offer:** `ai-fitness-vault.pdf` at $27 (list price), one-click checkout link with Blueprint-buyer discount code pre-applied.

**Body outline:**
- Hook: "By day 3 you've probably hit your first 'what if I can't find X food' moment."
- Context: explain the Vault is the ChatGPT prompt library built for exactly that.
- Proof: 3 specific prompts it includes (meal sub, deload calculator, travel-week generator).
- CTA: single button, checkout link.

## 3. Day 7 Kit Email — Inner Circle Subscription

**Trigger:** 7 days after Blueprint purchase. Kit tag `blueprint-buyer` AND NOT `inner-circle`.

**Subject:** "Week 1 done. Here is where most guys quit."

**Angle:** By day 7 most guys are feeling the first plateau anxiety. Inner Circle is the $27/mo continuity program that keeps them plugged in: weekly training update, monthly Q&A, new protocols as the author ships them.

**Offer:** Inner Circle — $27/mo, cancel anytime, first month at $9 for Blueprint buyers.

**Body outline:**
- Hook: "Week 1 is the dangerous week. You feel fine. That is not the same as improving."
- Problem: progress isn't linear; most guys stop tweaking after week 2.
- Solution: Inner Circle delivers a weekly training update email every Sunday — what to adjust, what to hold, what to watch for.
- Proof: example week 1 update (dummy content placeholder in Kit).
- CTA: "Try Inner Circle for $9 this month."

**Target LTV impact:** 15% conversion at $27/mo average retention 4 months = $16 incremental LTV per Blueprint buyer.

---

## 4. Abandoned-Cart Recovery (Kit automation)

**Trigger:** Clicked checkout link, did not complete purchase within 30 minutes.

### Email 1 — 1 hour after abandon

- Subject: "Left something on the table"
- Body: one-paragraph reminder of what the Blueprint includes, link back to checkout, no discount yet.

### Email 2 — 24 hours after abandon

- Subject: "The three reasons men over 35 plateau"
- Body: value-first, reiterate the Problem section from the sales page, end with CTA back to checkout.

### Email 3 — 72 hours after abandon

- Subject: "Last call — $20 off if you grab it today"
- Body: one-time $20-off code valid 24 hours, explicit urgency, link to checkout with code pre-applied.

**Kit personalizes the greeting and product title automatically. Bodies above are outlines, not final copy — Kit renders the final.**
