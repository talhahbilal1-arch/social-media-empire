FAQ

How long should curtains be?

Curtains should either just touch the floor (most common) or puddle 1-2 inches for a more formal look. Never above the floor unless they are cafe curtains. Too-short curtains are the most common window treatment mistake.

How wide should curtain panels be?

Each panel should be 1.5-2x the width of the window for proper fullness when closed. A 40-inch window needs panels totaling 60-80 inches wide. This creates the gathered, luxurious look rather than a flat, stretched appearance.
