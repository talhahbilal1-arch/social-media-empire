FAQ

Can floating shelves hold heavy items?

Quality floating shelves with proper wall anchors can hold 20-50 pounds depending on the model. Always use drywall anchors rated for the weight, or better yet, mount into wall studs. The BAYKA shelves hold up to 30 lbs each.

How do I hang floating shelves straight?

Use a level app on your phone if you do not have a bubble level. Mark both bracket positions before drilling. Start with one bracket, hang the shelf, check level, then mark the second bracket position. This prevents the most common mistake — shelves that slope to one side.
