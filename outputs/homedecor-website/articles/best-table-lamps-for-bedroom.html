FAQ

What color temperature is best for bedroom lamps?

2700K (warm white) is ideal for bedrooms. It mimics sunset light and promotes melatonin production. Avoid anything above 4000K in the bedroom — cool white light suppresses sleep hormones.

Should bedroom lamps match?

Matching lamps create a classic, symmetrical look. Mismatched lamps of similar height and scale create a more casual, collected feel. Either approach works — just maintain consistent height within 2 inches.
