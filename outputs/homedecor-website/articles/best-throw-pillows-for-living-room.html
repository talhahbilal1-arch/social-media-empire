FAQ

How many throw pillows should I put on a couch?

For a standard 3-seater sofa, 3-5 pillows is ideal. Use odd numbers for a more natural, casual look. Even numbers work for more formal, symmetrical arrangements. Never more than 5 — beyond that, there is no room to sit.

What size throw pillows work best?

18x18 inches is the most versatile size for standard sofas. Add one or two 20x20 inch pillows for depth, and consider a lumbar pillow (12x20) for visual variety and actual back support.

How often should I wash throw pillow covers?

Every 2-4 weeks for covers on frequently used furniture. Most quality covers are machine-washable on gentle cycle. Always check the care label and wash on cold to prevent color fading.
