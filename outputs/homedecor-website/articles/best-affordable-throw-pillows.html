FAQ

How many throw pillows should a sofa have?

3-5 pillows for most sofas. A 7-8 seat sectional can handle 6-8. The key is layering by size: larger pillows in back, medium in the middle, smaller in front. Odd numbers look more natural than even arrangements.

What size throw pillow is most versatile?

18x18 inches is the sweet spot. It works on most sofas and chairs without overpowering them. Pair with 12x20 lumbar pillows for complete coverage. 16x16 is also versatile but less commonly available.

The Takeaway

Throw pillows are the cheapest, fastest room refresh. Layer by size and texture — a mix of velvet, linen, and solid colors creates visual interest. Start with the Nestl set for quality and texture, then add 2-3 more in different styles. You'll transform your sofa for under $100.
