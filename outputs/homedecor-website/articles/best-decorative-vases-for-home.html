FAQ

How do I style vases on a shelf?

Use the rule of three: group vases in odd numbers with varying heights. Place the tallest in the back, medium in the middle, shortest in the front. Mix materials (ceramic + glass + woven) for visual interest.

What can I put in a vase besides flowers?

Dried pampas grass, eucalyptus stems, willow branches, cotton stems, wheat stalks, or decorative orbs. These last indefinitely without maintenance. For a modern look, leave statement vases completely empty.
