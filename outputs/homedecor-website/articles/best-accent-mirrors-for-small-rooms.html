FAQ

Where should I place a mirror to make a room look bigger?

Opposite a window to double the natural light, at the end of a narrow hallway to add perceived depth, or behind a table lamp to amplify light in dark corners. Avoid placing mirrors directly opposite each other — this creates a confusing visual loop.

What size mirror should I hang above a console table?

The mirror should be roughly 2/3 the width of the furniture below it. For a 48-inch console table, aim for a 30-36 inch wide mirror. Hang it 6-8 inches above the furniture surface.
