FAQ

What scent should I use in a bedroom?

Lavender, chamomile, vanilla, or soft florals. Avoid strong citrus or spicy scents — they're energizing. Bedroom candles should be calming. Light the candle 20 minutes before bed for maximum relaxation effect.

How long do scented candles actually last?

3-4 hours per burning session for most candles. Total burn time depends on wax type and size. Key tip: trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each use to maximize burn time and scent throw. A clean wick burns longer and cleaner.

The Takeaway

Don't buy candles randomly. Match the scent to the room and mood: lavender for bedrooms, fresh for kitchens, pine for focus. Invest in good soy wax candles from reputable brands. A $12-30 candle that burns properly lasts longer and smells better than cheaper alternatives.
