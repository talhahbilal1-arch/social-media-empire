FAQ

How do I keep woven baskets clean?

Vacuum with a brush attachment monthly. Spot clean with a damp cloth and mild soap. For deeper cleaning, wipe down with a vinegar-water solution and let air dry completely. Avoid submerging woven baskets in water.

What should I store in decorative baskets?

Blankets, throw pillows, toys, magazines, toiletries, cleaning supplies, scarves, and accessories. The rule: if it would create visual clutter sitting on a shelf, put it in a basket. The basket becomes the decor.
