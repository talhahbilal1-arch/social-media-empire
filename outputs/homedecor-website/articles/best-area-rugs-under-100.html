FAQ

What size area rug do I need for a living room?

The front legs of all major seating should sit on the rug at minimum. For most living rooms, an 8x10 rug is ideal. A 5x7 works for smaller spaces or under coffee tables only. When in doubt, go bigger.

How do I keep an area rug from sliding?

Use a non-slip rug pad. It keeps the rug in place, protects hardwood floors, and adds extra cushion. A quality rug pad costs $15-25 and extends the life of any rug by preventing friction wear on the underside.
