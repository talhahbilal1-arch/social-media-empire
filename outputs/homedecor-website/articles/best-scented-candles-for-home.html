FAQ

Are soy candles better than paraffin?

Soy candles burn cleaner, produce less soot, and are made from renewable soybeans. Paraffin candles offer stronger scent throw and are less expensive. For bedrooms and small spaces, soy is the better choice for air quality.

How long should I burn a candle at a time?

One hour per inch of diameter for the first burn to create a full melt pool and prevent tunneling. Maximum 4 hours per session. Always trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each burn for clean, even burning.
