FAQ

What size wall art for a living room?

For a wall above a sofa: 36-40 inches wide. For smaller walls: 24-30 inches. If hanging 2-3 pieces: space them 2 inches apart. The goal is filling 60-75% of the wall space without looking crowded.

How high should I hang wall art?

Center the art 57 inches from the floor (eye level). If hanging above furniture, keep 8-10 inches of space between the top of the furniture and the bottom of the art. Consistency makes rooms feel intentional.

The Takeaway

Blank walls feel unfinished. Start with one gallery-wrapped canvas ($40-50) or a framed print ($30-40). As you get comfortable, layer in a floating shelf or picture frame set to create a gallery wall. Wall art transforms a room faster and cheaper than any other design decision.
