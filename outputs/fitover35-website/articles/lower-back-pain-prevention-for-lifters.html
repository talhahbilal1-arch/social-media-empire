Common Questions

How important is core strength for lower back pain prevention?
Core strength is essential. A strong core helps stabilize your spine and reduces stress on your lower back during lifts.
What are some good warm-up exercises for lower back pain prevention?
Light cardio, dynamic stretching like leg swings and torso twists, and core activation exercises are all great options.
How often should I foam roll to prevent lower back pain?
Foam rolling 2-3 times per week can help release muscle tension and improve flexibility, contributing to lower back pain prevention.
