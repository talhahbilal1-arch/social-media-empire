Common Questions

Is creatine safe for men over 35?
Yes, creatine is generally safe for healthy men over 35. However, consult your doctor if you have any pre-existing kidney conditions.
Will creatine cause me to gain weight?
Creatine can cause a temporary increase in water weight. This is intracellular water retention, which can make muscles look fuller and does not equal fat gain.
How much creatine should I take?
A typical dose is 3-5 grams of creatine monohydrate per day. For HCL, follow the manufacturer's recommendations, as dosages may be lower.
