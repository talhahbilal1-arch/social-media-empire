Common Questions

Why is thoracic spine mobility important after 35?
As we age, our spines tend to stiffen. Improved mobility allows for better posture, reduced pain, and enhanced athletic performance.
How often should I do these exercises?
Aim for 3-5 times per week. Consistency is key to seeing lasting improvements in your posture and mobility.
Can poor thoracic mobility affect my lifts?
Absolutely. A stiff T-spine limits your overhead range of motion and can compromise form, increasing the risk of injury.
