Frequently Asked Questions

Why is recovery more important after 35?

Metabolism slows and hormone levels change, making muscle repair less efficient. Prioritizing recovery becomes essential for continued progress.

How often should I focus on active recovery?

Aim for at least 2-3 times per week. Light cardio, stretching, or yoga can significantly reduce muscle soreness.

What role does nutrition play in recovery?

Protein is crucial for muscle repair, while complex carbs replenish glycogen stores. Staying hydrated and consuming anti-inflammatory foods also helps.
