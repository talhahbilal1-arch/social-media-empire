Common Questions

How much protein should I consume post-workout?
Aim for 20-40 grams of high-quality protein within an hour after your workout to maximize muscle protein synthesis. This helps repair and rebuild muscle tissue.
Are carbs necessary after a workout?
Yes, especially after intense workouts. Carbs replenish glycogen stores, providing energy and aiding recovery. Choose complex carbs like oats or sweet potatoes.
What about creatine?
Creatine is beneficial for muscle strength and power. While not strictly post-workout, consistent creatine supplementation can enhance overall performance and recovery.
