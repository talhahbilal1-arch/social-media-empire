Common Questions

Should I load creatine when I first start taking it?
While a loading phase can saturate your muscles faster, it's not essential. Consistent daily intake of 3-5 grams will achieve the same results over time.
Is creatine safe for men over 35?
Yes, creatine is generally safe for healthy men over 35. However, it's always best to consult with your doctor before starting any new supplement, especially if you have pre-existing kidney conditions.
How much water should I drink when taking creatine?
Creatine draws water into your muscles, so it's important to stay well-hydrated. Aim for at least a gallon of water per day to support optimal creatine absorption and overall health.
