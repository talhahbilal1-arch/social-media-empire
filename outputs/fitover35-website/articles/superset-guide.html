Common Questions

What exactly is a superset?
A superset involves performing two exercises back-to-back with little to no rest. This technique increases workout intensity and saves time.
Are supersets good for building muscle?
Yes, supersets can be very effective for building muscle. They increase time under tension and metabolic stress, both crucial for hypertrophy.
How often should I incorporate supersets into my routine?
Start with 1-2 supersets per workout and gradually increase the frequency as you adapt. Listen to your body and avoid overtraining.
