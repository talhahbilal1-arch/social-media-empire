Common Questions

How much protein should I eat on a cutting diet?
Aim for 1-1.2 grams of protein per pound of bodyweight to preserve muscle mass during a calorie deficit. Spread it throughout the day.
What's the best way to track my calories and macros?
Use a food tracking app like MyFitnessPal or Lose It! to log your meals and monitor your progress. Consistency is key for accurate tracking.
Can I still eat carbs on a cutting diet?
Yes, but prioritize complex carbs and time them around your workouts for optimal energy and muscle recovery. Don't eliminate them entirely.
