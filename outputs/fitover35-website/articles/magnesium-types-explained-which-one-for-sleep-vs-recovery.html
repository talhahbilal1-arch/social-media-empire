Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to take magnesium for sleep?

Take Magnesium Glycinate about an hour before bed to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality. Consistency is key for optimal results.

Can I take different types of magnesium at the same time?

It's generally safe, but start with lower doses to assess tolerance. Consider taking Magnesium Malate earlier in the day and Glycinate before bed.

Are there any side effects of taking magnesium?

Some individuals may experience mild digestive upset, such as diarrhea, especially with higher doses. Start with a low dose and gradually increase as tolerated.
