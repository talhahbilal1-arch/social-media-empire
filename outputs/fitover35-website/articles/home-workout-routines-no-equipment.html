Common Questions

How often should I do bodyweight workouts?
Aim for 3-5 times per week, allowing for rest and recovery between sessions. Listen to your body and adjust as needed.
Can I build muscle with just bodyweight exercises?
Absolutely! Focus on progressive overload by increasing reps, sets, or difficulty of exercises. Nutrition is also key for muscle growth.
What if I have joint pain or injuries?
Modify exercises to reduce stress on affected joints, or consult with a physical therapist. There are always alternative exercises you can do!
