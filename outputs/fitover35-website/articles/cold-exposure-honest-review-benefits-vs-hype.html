Frequently Asked Questions

How cold should the water be for a cold plunge?

Aim for 50-59°F (10-15°C) for optimal benefits. Start with shorter durations and gradually increase as you adapt.

How long should I stay in a cold plunge?

Start with 1-2 minutes and gradually increase to 5-10 minutes. Listen to your body and get out if you feel uncomfortable.

What are the benefits of cold plunging?

Reported benefits include reduced muscle soreness, improved recovery, increased mental resilience, and enhanced immune function. Results vary, but consistency is key.
