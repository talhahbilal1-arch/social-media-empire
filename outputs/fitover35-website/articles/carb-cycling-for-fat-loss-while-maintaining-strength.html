Frequently Asked Questions

Will carb cycling automatically lead to fat loss?

Not necessarily. Carb cycling is a tool, but you still need to be in a calorie deficit for fat loss to occur. Monitor your overall calorie intake.

How do I determine my high-carb and low-carb days?

Base it on your training schedule. High-carb days should coincide with your heaviest lifting days, while low-carb days can be on rest or light activity days.

Can I still eat my favorite foods while carb cycling?

Yes, but moderation is key. Focus on nutrient-dense sources of carbs on high-carb days and prioritize protein and healthy fats on low-carb days.
