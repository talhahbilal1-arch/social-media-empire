Common Questions

Is 3 days a week really enough for a full body workout?
Yes, research shows that training each muscle group 2-3 times per week optimizes muscle protein synthesis. Focus on intensity and proper form.
What if I miss a workout day?
Don't stress! Just get back on track with the next scheduled workout. Consistency over time is more important than perfection.
How long should each workout session last?
Aim for 45-60 minutes, including warm-up and cool-down. Prioritize compound exercises for maximum efficiency.
