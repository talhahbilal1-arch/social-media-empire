Over weeks and months, those extra reps and heavier lifts add up to real gains in strength and muscle mass. You’ll also notice your muscles look a bit fuller, as creatine draws water into the muscle cells.

**My take:** This is my number one recommendation for any guy looking to build muscle. It's cheap, safe, and effective. I take 5 grams every day, mixed into my post-workout shake or even just water. Don’t overthink the "loading phase" – consistent daily intake works fine.
