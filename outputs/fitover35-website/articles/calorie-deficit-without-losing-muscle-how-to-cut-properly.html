Frequently Asked Questions

How much protein should I eat to preserve muscle while cutting?

Aim for at least 1 gram of protein per pound of bodyweight daily. Distribute your protein intake evenly throughout the day.

Is cardio necessary for fat loss?

Cardio isn't strictly necessary, but it can help create a larger calorie deficit. Focus on resistance training to maintain muscle mass.

What's the best way to track my calorie intake?

Use a food tracking app like MyFitnessPal to monitor your calorie and macronutrient intake. Consistency is key for accurate tracking.
