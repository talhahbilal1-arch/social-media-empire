Common Questions

How much sleep do I really need to optimize testosterone?
Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night. Inconsistent sleep patterns can significantly impact hormone production.
What are some other ways to improve sleep quality besides these products?
Establish a consistent sleep schedule, create a relaxing bedtime routine, and optimize your sleep environment for darkness and coolness. Consider limiting caffeine and alcohol intake before bed.
Can sleep apnea affect testosterone levels?
Yes, sleep apnea can severely disrupt sleep and negatively impact testosterone. Consult a doctor if you suspect you may have sleep apnea.
