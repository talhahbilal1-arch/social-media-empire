Common Questions

How much protein should I consume daily for weight loss?
Aim for 0.8-1 gram of protein per pound of body weight daily. This helps preserve muscle mass while you lose weight.
When is the best time to take protein powder for weight loss?
Consider consuming protein powder post-workout to aid muscle recovery or as a meal replacement to control calorie intake. Experiment to find what works best for your schedule.
Are there any risks associated with high protein intake?
For most healthy individuals, high protein intake is safe. However, it's important to stay hydrated and consult with a doctor if you have kidney issues.
