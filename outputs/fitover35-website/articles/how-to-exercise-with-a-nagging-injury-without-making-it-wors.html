Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really exercise with an injury, or should I just rest?

Rest is important initially, but strategic exercise can often promote healing by improving circulation and strengthening surrounding muscles. Consult your doctor first!

What kind of exercises should I avoid?

Avoid high-impact activities, exercises that cause sharp pain, and anything that puts direct stress on the injured area. Focus on controlled movements and listen to your body.

How do I know if I'm pushing myself too hard?

Pain is your guide. If you experience increasing pain during or after exercise, reduce the intensity or stop altogether. Slight discomfort is normal, but sharp, stabbing pain is a warning sign.
