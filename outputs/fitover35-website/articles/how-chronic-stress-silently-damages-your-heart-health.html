Frequently Asked Questions

Can stress really damage my heart?

Yes, chronic stress elevates blood pressure, increases inflammation, and can lead to serious heart conditions like heart disease and stroke. It's important to manage it proactively.

What are some other ways to manage stress besides supplements and apps?

Regular exercise, a healthy diet, spending time in nature, and connecting with loved ones are all excellent ways to reduce stress and improve your overall well-being. Prioritize activities that bring you joy and relaxation.

How much magnesium should I take?

The recommended daily dose of magnesium varies, but most adults benefit from 200-400mg of magnesium glycinate. Consult with your doctor to determine the right dosage for your individual needs.
