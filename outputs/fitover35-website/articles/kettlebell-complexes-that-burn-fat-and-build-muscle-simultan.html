Common Questions

How often should I do kettlebell complexes?
Aim for 2-3 times per week, allowing for adequate recovery between sessions. Listen to your body and adjust as needed.
What weight kettlebell should I use?
Start with a weight that allows you to maintain good form throughout the entire complex. Men typically start with 16-24kg.
Are kettlebell complexes better than traditional weightlifting?
They offer a different approach, emphasizing conditioning and functional movement. Best to incorporate both for well-rounded fitness.
