Common Questions

Will intermittent fasting cause muscle loss after 35?
It's possible, but preventable with adequate protein intake during eating windows and strategic workout timing. Prioritize resistance training and monitor your body composition.
Is IF safe for men over 35 with existing health conditions?
It depends on the condition. Consult your doctor before starting IF, especially if you have diabetes, heart issues, or are taking medication.
What's the best intermittent fasting schedule for men over 35 who lift weights?
A 16/8 schedule (16 hours fasting, 8 hours eating) often works well. Time your workouts towards the end of your fasting window or at the beginning of your eating window to optimize performance and recovery.
