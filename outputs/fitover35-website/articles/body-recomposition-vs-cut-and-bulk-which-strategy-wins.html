Common Questions

Is body recomposition possible after 35?
Yes, it's absolutely possible! It requires a consistent approach to diet and exercise, focusing on building muscle and losing fat simultaneously.
Which is better: bulk/cut or body recomposition?
For men over 35, body recomposition is often better as it's more sustainable and less stressful on the body. Bulk/cut cycles can be harsh.
How long does body recomposition take?
It's a slower process than bulk/cut, typically taking several months to see noticeable results. Consistency is key for long-term success.
