Common Questions

How much protein should men over 50 consume daily?
Aim for 0.8-1.2 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight daily. This helps maintain muscle mass and supports overall health as you age.
Why is low sugar important in protein powder for older men?
Excess sugar can contribute to weight gain and insulin resistance, common concerns for men over 50. Low-sugar options support healthy blood sugar levels and weight management.
Are there any potential side effects of protein powder?
Some individuals may experience digestive issues like bloating or gas. Start with a small dose and gradually increase as tolerated. Consult with a doctor if you have concerns.
