Frequently Asked Questions

Why is stretching important for older men?

Stretching improves flexibility, reduces muscle soreness, and prevents injuries. It's crucial for maintaining mobility as we age.

How often should I stretch?

Aim for at least 2-3 times per week, focusing on major muscle groups. Consistency is key for seeing results.

What's the best time to stretch?

Post-workout is ideal when muscles are warm and pliable. You can also stretch on rest days to maintain flexibility.
