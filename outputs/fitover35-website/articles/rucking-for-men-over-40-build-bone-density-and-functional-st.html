Common Questions

How much weight should a man over 40 ruck with?
Start with 10-20 pounds and gradually increase the weight as your strength and endurance improve. Listen to your body and prioritize proper form.
What are the best shoes for rucking?
Trail running shoes or hiking boots with good support and traction are ideal. Avoid minimalist shoes unless you're already accustomed to them.
How often should I ruck to improve bone density?
Aim for 2-3 rucking sessions per week, lasting 30-60 minutes each. Consistency is key to seeing results.
