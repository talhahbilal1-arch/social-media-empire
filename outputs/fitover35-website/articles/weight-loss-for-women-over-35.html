Common Questions

Is it harder to lose weight after 35?
Yes, metabolism naturally slows down with age. Focusing on strength training and a protein-rich diet can help combat this.
What are the best exercises for weight loss after 35?
A combination of strength training (like lifting weights or using resistance bands) and cardio (like walking, running, or swimming) is ideal. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week.
How important is diet for weight loss after 35?
Diet is crucial! Focus on whole, unprocessed foods, plenty of protein, and mindful portion control. Avoid sugary drinks and processed snacks.
