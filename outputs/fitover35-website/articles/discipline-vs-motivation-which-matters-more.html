Common Questions

Is motivation completely useless?
Not at all! Motivation can be a great starting point, but it's unreliable for long-term success. Use it to kickstart your discipline.
How do I start building discipline?
Start small. Commit to one small, achievable fitness goal each day, like a 15-minute walk or five push-ups. Consistency is key.
What if I miss a workout?
Don't beat yourself up. Acknowledge it, learn from it, and get back on track with your next scheduled workout. One missed workout doesn't negate your progress.
