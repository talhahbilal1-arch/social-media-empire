Common Questions

How can I stay motivated when I'm short on time?
Focus on micro-workouts: 10-15 minute bursts of exercise throughout the day. Combine it with habit stacking, linking a new exercise to something you already do.
What supplements are most effective for men over 35?
Creatine and whey protein are well-researched and effective for building muscle and improving recovery. Consider consulting a doctor before starting any new supplement regimen.
How much exercise do I really need each week?
Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity, plus strength training at least twice a week. Even short, consistent sessions can make a big difference.
