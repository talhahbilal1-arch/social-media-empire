Frequently Asked Questions

Why is deadlift form so important for people with desk jobs?

Prolonged sitting weakens postural muscles, making proper form even more crucial to prevent back injuries. Correct form ensures you're engaging the right muscles.

How often should I deadlift if I have a desk job?

Start with 1-2 times per week, focusing on perfect form and gradually increasing weight. Listen to your body and prioritize recovery.

What are some warning signs that my deadlift form is off?

Lower back pain, rounding of the back, and difficulty maintaining a neutral spine are all red flags. Reduce the weight and focus on form or seek professional guidance.
