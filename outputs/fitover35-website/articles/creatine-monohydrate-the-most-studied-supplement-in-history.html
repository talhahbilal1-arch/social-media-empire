Common Questions

Should I take creatine and protein powder together?
Yes, they can be taken together. Creatine enhances performance, while protein supports muscle recovery and growth.
Is creatine safe for men over 35?
Creatine is generally safe for healthy adults. However, consult with your doctor before starting any new supplement regimen.
Will creatine make me gain weight?
Creatine may cause some water retention, leading to a temporary increase in weight. This is not the same as fat gain.
