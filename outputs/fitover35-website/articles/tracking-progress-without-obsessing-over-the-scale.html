Frequently Asked Questions

Why shouldn't I rely solely on the scale after 35?

Muscle mass decreases with age, and the scale doesn't differentiate between muscle and fat. Focus on body composition and performance metrics instead.

How often should I take body measurements?

Once a month is sufficient to track progress without obsessing. Consistency is key, so use the same tape measure and measurement points each time.

What other metrics should I track besides weight and measurements?

Track your strength gains (weight lifted), endurance (cardio time), energy levels, and sleep quality. These are all indicators of overall fitness.
