Common Questions

How often should I workout in my home gym?
Aim for 3-4 sessions per week, focusing on compound exercises. Ensure adequate rest and recovery between sessions.
What are the most effective exercises with limited equipment?
Focus on squats, push-ups, rows (with bands or dumbbells), and lunges. Prioritize proper form and progressive overload.
How important is warming up before a home workout?
Warming up is crucial to prevent injuries and prime your muscles. Spend 5-10 minutes on light cardio and dynamic stretching before each workout.
