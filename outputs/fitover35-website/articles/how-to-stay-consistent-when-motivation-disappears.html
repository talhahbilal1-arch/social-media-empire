Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between motivation and discipline?

Motivation is a feeling; discipline is a choice. Discipline is about consistently taking action even when you don't feel motivated.

How long does it take to form a habit?

While the popular myth is 21 days, research suggests it varies greatly, from 18 to 254 days. Focus on consistency, not a specific timeline.

What if I miss a workout?

Don't beat yourself up! Acknowledge it, learn from it, and get back on track with your next scheduled workout. Consistency is about the long game.
