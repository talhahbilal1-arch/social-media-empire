Common Questions

How much space do I need for a basic home gym?
A space of roughly 6x8 feet is sufficient for a power rack and some free weights. Adjust based on your specific equipment needs.
What's the most important piece of equipment to start with?
A power rack is the best starting point for serious lifting. It provides safety and versatility for compound exercises.
How can I stay motivated to work out at home?
Set realistic goals, create a workout schedule, and find an accountability partner. Tracking your progress can also be motivating.
