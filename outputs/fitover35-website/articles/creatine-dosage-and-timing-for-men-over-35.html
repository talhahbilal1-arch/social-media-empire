Common Questions

Should I load creatine after 35?
Loading creatine isn't necessary, and for older lifters, it can increase the risk of digestive discomfort. Stick to a consistent daily dose of 3-5 grams.
Is creatine safe for older men?
Creatine is generally safe for healthy adults, including those over 35. However, consult your doctor, especially if you have pre-existing kidney or liver conditions.
How much creatine should I take daily after 35?
A daily dose of 3-5 grams of creatine monohydrate is generally recommended for maintaining optimal muscle creatine stores and performance benefits.
