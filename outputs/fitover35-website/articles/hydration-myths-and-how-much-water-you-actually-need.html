Common Questions

How much water should I drink daily after 35?
A good starting point is half your body weight in ounces, but adjust based on activity level and climate. Pay attention to your thirst cues and urine color.
Are sports drinks better than water for hydration?
Not necessarily. Sports drinks can be beneficial during intense, prolonged exercise, but water is often sufficient for most workouts and daily hydration. Choose wisely based on your needs.
Can I overhydrate?
Yes, overhydration (hyponatremia) can occur, especially during endurance events. Listen to your body and avoid excessive fluid intake, particularly without electrolytes.
