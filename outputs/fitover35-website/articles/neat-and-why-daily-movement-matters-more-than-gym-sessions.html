Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is NEAT movement?

NEAT, or Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis, encompasses all the physical activity you do that isn't formal exercise, like walking, fidgeting, and even standing. It's a significant contributor to daily calorie burn.

Can I lose fat just by walking?

While walking alone might not be enough for dramatic fat loss, consistently incorporating it into your routine significantly boosts your overall calorie expenditure. Combine it with a healthy diet and strength training for optimal results.

How much walking is considered effective for fat loss?

Aim for at least 7,000-10,000 steps per day to maximize the fat-burning benefits of NEAT. Track your progress with a pedometer or fitness tracker and gradually increase your daily steps.
