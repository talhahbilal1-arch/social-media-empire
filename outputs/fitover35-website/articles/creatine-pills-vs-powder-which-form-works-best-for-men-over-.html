Common Questions

Are creatine pills or powder better for absorption?
Powder generally absorbs slightly faster due to its readily dissolvable nature. However, micronized creatine in both forms can minimize the difference.
Is creatine safe for men over 35?
Yes, creatine is generally safe for healthy men over 35. Consult your doctor if you have pre-existing kidney conditions or other health concerns.
How much creatine should I take daily?
A typical daily dose is 3-5 grams. Some people 'load' with a higher dose initially, but it's not strictly necessary for benefits.
