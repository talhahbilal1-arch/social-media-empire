Common Questions

How much protein do men over 50 need?
Aim for 0.8-1.0 grams of protein per pound of body weight daily. This supports muscle maintenance and overall health.
What are the benefits of protein powder for older men?
Protein powder helps maintain muscle mass, supports bone health, and boosts energy levels. It's a convenient way to meet your daily protein needs.
Are there any risks to taking protein powder?
Generally, protein powder is safe when consumed in moderation. However, excessive intake can strain the kidneys and liver, so consult with your doctor if you have pre-existing conditions.
