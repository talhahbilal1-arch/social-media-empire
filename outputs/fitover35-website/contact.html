Get in Touch

Whether you have a question about training, want to discuss coaching options, or have feedback on the content, I'd like to hear from you. I respond to every message personally, typically within 48 hours.

What I Can Help With

  • Training Questions: Specific questions about programming, exercise selection, or training principles.
  • Coaching Inquiries: Information about one-on-one coaching availability and approach.
  • Content Feedback: Suggestions for articles or feedback on existing content.
  • Collaboration: Podcast appearances, guest posts, or other professional opportunities.

Response Time

I aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate that in your subject line.

Common Questions

Do you offer online coaching?

Yes. I work with a limited number of clients on a one-on-one basis. Coaching includes customized programming, nutrition guidance, and ongoing support. Availability varies. Send a message through the form above to discuss whether it's a good fit.

Can you review my current program?

I don't offer free program reviews, but I'm happy to answer specific questions about training principles that might help you evaluate your own program. For detailed program analysis, that falls under coaching services.

Do you have a training program I can follow?

Not currently. The articles here provide principles you can apply to any program. I'm working on structured programming options for the future. Join the newsletter to be notified when they're available.

Are you available for podcasts or interviews?

Selectively, yes. I'm most interested in conversations that go beyond surface-level fitness topics into the psychology of change, systems thinking, and long-term mastery. Send details about your show and audience through the contact form.
