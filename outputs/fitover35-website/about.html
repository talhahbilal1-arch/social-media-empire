About FitOver35

FitOver35 exists for one reason: to give men over 35 the straight-talking, evidence-based fitness information they actually need — without the hype, bro-science, or programs designed for 22-year-olds.

Every article on this site is researched, written, and reviewed by an ISSA Certified Personal Trainer with 6+ years of hands-on coaching experience. We specialize in the unique challenges men face after 35 — declining testosterone, slower recovery, joint wear, career-induced stress, and the reality that training needs to be efficient when life gets busy.

What We Stand For

Evidence Over Opinion
Every recommendation is backed by peer-reviewed research or real-world data from coaching hundreds of men. If the science doesn't support it, we don't recommend it.
Honesty Over Hype
We'll tell you when a supplement is a waste of money. We'll tell you when the "new revolutionary method" is just repackaged basics. You deserve the truth.
Systems Over Motivation
Motivation fades. Systems don't. We focus on building repeatable training and nutrition systems that work even on the days you don't feel like it.
Longevity Over Quick Fixes
Our programs are designed for the long game — building a body that performs well at 45, 55, and beyond. Not just a 6-week transformation for Instagram.

How We Make Money

FitOver35 is supported by affiliate commissions. When we recommend a product, we link to it with an affiliate tag. If you purchase through our link, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This never influences our recommendations. We regularly recommend budget options and free alternatives when they're the best choice.
