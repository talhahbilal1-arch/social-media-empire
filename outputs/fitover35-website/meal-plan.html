Free 7-Day High-Protein Meal Plan

Simple meals, real food, no complicated recipes. 2,200-2,500 calories and 180g+ protein per day — built for men over 35 who lift.

The rules: 30g+ protein at every meal. Eat whole foods 80% of the time. No calorie counting required — the structure handles it. Adjust portions up or down based on your goal (muscle gain vs. fat loss).

The 7-Day Plan

Day 1 — Monday
Breakfast
3 eggs scrambled + 2 turkey sausage links + 1 slice whole grain toast + handful spinach
~35g protein
Lunch
Grilled chicken breast (6oz) + mixed greens + avocado + olive oil dressing + rice (1 cup cooked)
~45g protein

Greek yogurt (1 cup) + handful of almonds + berries

~30g protein

~48g protein

~42g protein

Casein protein + peanut butter (1 tbsp)
~30g protein
