FAQ

When should I apply vitamin C serum?

Apply in the morning after cleansing, before moisturizer and SPF. Vitamin C provides antioxidant sun protection when paired with SPF 30+. Wait 1-2 minutes for absorption before layering other products.

Can I use vitamin C with retinol?

Not at the same time. Both are powerful actives. Use vitamin C in your AM routine and retinol at night. This maximizes benefits without causing irritation. Some advanced users do mix them, but beginners should keep them separate.

How long until vitamin C serum works?

4-6 weeks for visible results. You'll notice brighter skin within 2 weeks, but true brightening and fine line reduction takes a full 6 weeks. Consistency matters more than concentration.
