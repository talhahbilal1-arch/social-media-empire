FAQ

How often should I clean my makeup brushes?

Ideally once a week for brushes used daily. At minimum, every two weeks. Dirty brushes harbor bacteria that cause breakouts, and product buildup prevents smooth application. Use baby shampoo or brush cleanser.

Synthetic vs natural hair brushes — which is better?

Synthetic brushes are better for liquid and cream products and easier to clean. Natural hair brushes pick up and distribute powder products more evenly. For beginners, synthetic is the way to go — they are more versatile, vegan, and easier to maintain.
