Best Mascara for Volume and Length: 5 Mascaras Ranked After 30-Day Wear Tests

Mascara is the one makeup product most people refuse to leave the house without. It opens up the eyes, defines your face, and takes 30 seconds to apply. But finding a mascara that volumizes without clumping, lengthens without spider legs, and lasts all day without flaking is surprisingly hard.

I wore each of these mascaras for at least five full days, evaluating first application, midday hold, end-of-day flaking, and removal ease. The results surprised me — the best performance did not always come from the most expensive option.

FAQ

How often should I replace my mascara?

Every 3 months maximum. Mascara harbors bacteria faster than any other makeup product because the wand goes in and out of the tube, introducing air and bacteria with each use. If it smells off or the formula has dried out, replace it immediately.

How do I prevent mascara from smudging?

Use a waterproof formula or layer a waterproof mascara over your regular mascara on the tips only. Set under-eye concealer with translucent powder to create a barrier. Avoid touching your eyes throughout the day.
