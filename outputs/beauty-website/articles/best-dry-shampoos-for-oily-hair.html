FAQ

Can dry shampoo damage your hair?

Used as directed (2-3 times per week maximum), dry shampoo does not damage hair. Overuse can clog hair follicles and cause scalp irritation. Always wash with regular shampoo at least 2-3 times per week.

Why does my dry shampoo leave white residue?

You are either spraying too close or not massaging it in enough. Hold the can 8-10 inches from roots, spray in short bursts, wait 30 seconds, then massage thoroughly with fingertips. For dark hair, look for tinted or translucent formulas.
