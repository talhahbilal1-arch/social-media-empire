FAQ

How often should I use a jade roller?

Daily is safe and recommended. Morning use is most effective for depuffing overnight fluid retention. Use after applying serum or moisturizer — never on bare, dry skin. Each session takes 3-5 minutes.

Real jade vs fake — how can I tell?

Real jade feels cold to the touch and warms slowly. It has natural color variations and imperfections. Fake jade (dyed glass) feels lighter, looks too uniform in color, and warms quickly. The BAIMEI and Mount Lai sets both use verified real jade.
