FAQ

Do I need sunscreen indoors?

If you sit near windows, yes. UVA rays penetrate glass and cause photoaging. If you work from home near a window, daily sunscreen is still important. If you are in an interior room all day, you can skip it.

How much sunscreen should I apply?

Two finger-lengths for face and neck. Most people apply only 25-50% of the recommended amount, which dramatically reduces protection. Reapply every 2 hours when outdoors, or after sweating or swimming.

Chemical vs mineral sunscreen — which is better?

Both are effective and safe. Chemical sunscreens absorb UV, mineral sunscreens reflect it. Chemical formulas tend to be more cosmetically elegant (less white cast). Mineral is better for very sensitive skin or if you prefer fewer synthetic ingredients.
