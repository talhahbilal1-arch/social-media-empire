FAQ

How often should beginners use retinol?

Start with 2x/week (Monday and Thursday nights, 3+ days apart). After 2-3 weeks of no irritation, increase to 3x/week. Only move to nightly use after 6-8 weeks of regular use. Going too fast causes redness and peeling that scares people away from retinol permanently.

Can I use retinol in summer?

Yes, absolutely — but use SPF 30+ daily (non-negotiable). Retinol makes skin more sun-sensitive, so sun protection is critical. Summer is actually fine as long as you're vigilant with sunscreen. Avoid during beach vacations where sun exposure is extreme.

What is retinol vs retinoids?

Retinol is OTC and converts to retinoic acid in your skin (slower, gentler). Retinoids are prescription-strength (tretinoins like Retin-A). Start with OTC retinol, graduate to prescription retinoids if you need stronger results after 3-6 months.
