FAQ

How long does vitamin C serum take to show results?

Most people see initial brightening within 2-3 weeks. Significant improvements in dark spots and uneven tone take 6-8 weeks of consistent daily use. Patience and consistency matter more than concentration.

Can I use vitamin C serum with retinol?

Yes, but use them at different times. Vitamin C in the morning (it provides antioxidant protection from UV), retinol at night (it increases sun sensitivity). Using both simultaneously can cause irritation.

How do I know if my vitamin C serum has gone bad?

It turns dark orange or brown. Fresh L-ascorbic acid serum should be clear to pale yellow. Once it darkens significantly, it has oxidized and lost most of its efficacy. Store in a cool, dark place to extend shelf life.
