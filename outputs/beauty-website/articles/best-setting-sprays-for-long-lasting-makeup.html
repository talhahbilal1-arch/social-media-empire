FAQ

How do I apply setting spray correctly?

Hold 8-10 inches from face. Spray in an X pattern, then a T pattern. Do not rub or touch your face — let it air dry for 30 seconds. Two light coats are better than one heavy coat.

Setting spray vs setting powder — do I need both?

Setting powder sets cream and liquid products. Setting spray locks everything in place and prevents transfer. For the longest wear, use both: powder first on the T-zone, then spray over everything.
