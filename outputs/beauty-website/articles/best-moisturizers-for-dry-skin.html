Best Moisturizers for Dry Skin in 2026: 5 Creams That End the Flaking for Good

Most moisturizers just sit on top of dry skin and create the illusion of hydration for a few hours. Then the tightness returns, the flaking comes back, and you apply more. It is a cycle that benefits moisturizer companies but not your skin.

The fix is not more moisturizer — it is the right moisturizer. Dry skin needs ingredients that actually repair the moisture barrier (ceramides, cholesterol, fatty acids) combined with humectants that pull water into the skin. FAQ

Why is my skin still dry even though I moisturize?

Three common reasons: you are applying to fully dry skin (apply to damp skin instead), your cleanser is stripping your moisture barrier (switch to a gentle, non-foaming cleanser), or you are not using enough product. You need a nickel-sized amount for the face.

Should I use a different moisturizer in winter?

Yes, most people benefit from a richer moisturizer in winter when humidity drops. Switch from a gel or lotion to a cream formula (like CeraVe Moisturizing Cream) during cold, dry months.
