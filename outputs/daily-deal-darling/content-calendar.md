# Daily Deal Darling - 30-Day Pinterest Content Calendar

**Brand:** Daily Deal Darling
**Target Audience:** Budget-conscious women 25-45
**Calendar Period:** Days 1-30
**Total Pins Planned:** 240 (8 pins/day x 30 days)
**Posting Schedule:** 8am, 10am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm, 6pm, 8pm, 10pm PST

---

## Content Strategy Overview

### Daily Content Mix
| Content Type | Pins/Day | Focus Areas |
|--------------|----------|-------------|
| Home/Organization | 3 | Decluttering, storage hacks, cleaning tips, home decor on a budget |
| Self-Care/Wellness | 2 | Mental health, fitness tips, healthy recipes, stress relief |
| Beauty/Lifestyle | 2 | Skincare routines, makeup dupes, fashion finds, style tips |
| Deal/Product | 1 | Product recommendations, deal alerts, budget finds |

### Board Strategy
**Primary Boards (rotate content across):**
1. **Budget Home Hacks** - Home organization, cleaning, decor
2. **Self-Care Sunday** - Wellness, mental health, relaxation
3. **Beauty on a Budget** - Skincare, makeup, beauty tips
4. **Healthy Living** - Recipes, fitness, wellness tips
5. **Style Steals** - Fashion, outfit ideas, wardrobe tips
6. **Daily Deals & Finds** - Product recommendations, sales alerts
7. **Organization Obsessed** - Storage, decluttering, systems
8. **Mom Life Hacks** - Family tips, time-savers, meal prep

### Weekly Themes
| Week | Theme | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| Week 1 (Days 1-7) | Fresh Start | New beginnings, decluttering, goal-setting |
| Week 2 (Days 8-14) | Self-Care Focus | Wellness routines, mental health, relaxation |
| Week 3 (Days 15-21) | Home Refresh | Organization projects, cleaning deep-dives |
| Week 4 (Days 22-28) | Beauty & Style | Skincare, fashion, personal care |
| Days 29-30 | Month Recap | Best-of content, community favorites |

### Time Slot Strategy
| Time (PST) | Best Content Type | Reasoning |
|------------|-------------------|-----------|
| 8am | Home/Organization | Morning motivation, start-the-day energy |
| 10am | Self-Care/Wellness | Mid-morning wellness inspiration |
| 12pm | Beauty/Lifestyle | Lunch break browsing, beauty content |
| 2pm | Home/Organization | Afternoon productivity boost |
| 4pm | Deal/Product | Pre-evening shopping mindset |
| 6pm | Beauty/Lifestyle | Evening routine inspiration |
| 8pm | Self-Care/Wellness | Wind-down content, relaxation |
| 10pm | Home/Organization | Planning for tomorrow |

### Weekend Considerations
- **Saturdays:** Focus on project-based content (organization projects, meal prep, DIY)
- **Sundays:** Emphasize self-care, relaxation, and week-ahead planning
- Weekend pins tend to have higher engagement - schedule high-value content
