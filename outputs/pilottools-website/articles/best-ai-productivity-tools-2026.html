FAQ

Do AI productivity tools actually save time?

Yes. The tools on this list save measurable hours per week when used for the right tasks. The key is using them for repetitive, mechanical tasks (transcription, scheduling, summarization) and human intelligence for strategic work.

What is the best AI assistant for work?

It depends on your job. Writers use Claude or ChatGPT. Researchers use Perplexity. Teams use Notion AI. Knowledge workers use Otter.ai for notes. Most productive teams combine 2-3 tools instead of relying on one.

The Takeaway

Start with one tool that fits your biggest pain point. If you're drowning in meeting notes: Otter.ai. If you're in Notion constantly: Notion AI. If scheduling is a nightmare: Reclaim.ai. Once you see time savings, stack a second tool. The compound effect of 2-3 productivity tools saves 5-8 hours/week.
