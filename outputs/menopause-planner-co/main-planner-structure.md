## PAGE 3: How to Use This Planner

### Layout
- Numbered sections with icon indicators
- Sage Green section dividers
- Tip boxes in Dusty Rose

### Text Content

**Header** (Deep Sage):
```
How to Use This Planner
```

**Introduction**:
```
This planner is designed to be flexible. Use it daily, weekly, or whenever you need it. There's no right or wrong way‚Äîonly your way.
```

**Section 1: Getting Started**
```
1. START WITH YOUR SYMPTOM TRACKER
   Begin by identifying which symptoms affect you most. Not every woman experiences the same symptoms, and yours may change over time.

2. TRACK DAILY OR WEEKLY
   Some women prefer daily logging; others find weekly reviews more sustainable. Do what works for your lifestyle.

3. LOOK FOR PATTERNS
   After 2-3 weeks of tracking, review your entries. You may notice:
   - Time-of-day patterns
   - Food or activity triggers
   - Stress connections
   - Sleep quality impacts
```

**Section 2: Section Guide**
```
SYMPTOM TRACKER (Pages 5-10)
Log your daily symptoms with intensity ratings. Includes space for notes on potential triggers.

HOT FLASH LOG (Pages 11-14)
Detailed tracking specifically for hot flashes and night sweats, including duration, intensity, and circumstances.

MOOD TRACKER (Pages 15-18)
Monitor emotional wellbeing, energy levels, and mental clarity throughout your cycle or month.

SLEEP LOG (Pages 19-22)
Track sleep quality, night wakings, and factors affecting your rest.

MEDICATION TRACKER (Pages 23-24)
Keep all supplements, medications, and dosages in one organized place.

DOCTOR VISIT NOTES (Pages 25-26)
Prepare for appointments and record important information from your healthcare providers.

SELF-CARE PLANNER (Pages 27-30)
Plan and prioritize activities that nourish your body, mind, and spirit.

MONTHLY REVIEWS (Pages 31-34)
Reflect on patterns, celebrate progress, and set intentions for the month ahead.
```

## DESIGN NOTES FOR PRODUCTION

### Typography Recommendations
- **Headers**: Elegant serif (e.g., Playfair Display, Cormorant)
- **Body text**: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Lato, Open Sans)
- **Accent text**: Script font sparingly (e.g., for quotes)

### Decorative Elements
- Botanical illustrations (eucalyptus, lavender, sage)
- Soft watercolor washes in corner accents
- Thin line dividers in Sage Green (#A5A58D)
- Rounded corners on boxes
- Subtle drop shadows on entry boxes

### Print Specifications
- Page size: 8.5" x 11" (Letter)
- Margins: 0.75" all sides
- Binding edge: Additional 0.25" gutter
- Paper recommendation: 70-80lb uncoated text

### Accessibility Considerations
- Minimum 11pt font for body text
- Sufficient contrast between text and background
- Ample writing space in all fields
- Clear visual hierarchy
