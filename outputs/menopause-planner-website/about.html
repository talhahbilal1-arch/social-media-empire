About Us

Real information. No fear. Just clarity.

Our Mission

The Menopause Planner exists to help women navigate one of life's most significant transitions with real information, not fear. Menopause doesn't have to be confusing—and it definitely doesn't have to be something you suffer through alone.

We believe every woman deserves evidence-based information about what's happening in her body, her options for managing symptoms, and practical strategies for thriving during menopause. Our goal is to empower you to make informed decisions about your health and your future.

What We Provide

We create clear, science-backed guides on menopause symptoms, hormone replacement therapy, natural alternatives, and lifestyle approaches. Every article is grounded in research, written in plain language, and designed to help you understand your body and your options.

Whether you're in perimenopause wondering what hot flashes are really about, or in full menopause looking for symptom management strategies, you'll find practical, honest information here.

Our Values

Evidence-Based

All our information is grounded in current research and medical understanding.

Honest

We tell you the good, the challenging, and the nuanced reality of menopause.

Empowering

Supportive

You're not alone in this. We're here to support your journey.

Why This Matters

Menopause affects how you sleep, your energy, your mood, your body, and your sense of self. That's why accurate information matters. That's why you deserve clarity instead of confusion. That's why The Menopause Planner exists—to meet you where you are and help you navigate this transition with confidence.
