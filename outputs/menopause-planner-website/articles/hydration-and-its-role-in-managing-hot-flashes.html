Frequently Asked Questions

How much water should I drink to reduce hot flashes?

Aim for at least 8 glasses (64 ounces) of water daily. Adjust based on your activity level and climate.

What's the best time to drink water for hot flash relief?

Sipping water throughout the day is more effective than chugging it all at once. Focus on hydrating consistently, especially before and during periods when you're prone to hot flashes.

Are there other drinks besides water that can help?

Herbal teas like chamomile or peppermint can be soothing, but avoid sugary drinks and caffeine, as they can worsen hot flashes.
