FAQ

Is melatonin safe for long-term menopause sleep?

Yes, at low doses (1-3mg). Melatonin is naturally produced by your body and supplementing at 5mg or less is safe for extended use. No dependency develops. It works best when paired with sleep hygiene (cool room, no screens 1 hour before bed).

Do weighted blankets actually help sleep?

Yes. The gentle pressure activates your parasympathetic nervous system (the rest-and-relax response), reducing cortisol and anxiety. Research shows weighted blankets help with both sleep initiation and sleep quality. Use 10% of body weight — a 150 lb woman needs a 15 lb blanket.
