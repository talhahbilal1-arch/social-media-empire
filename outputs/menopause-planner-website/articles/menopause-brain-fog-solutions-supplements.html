Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is menopause brain fog?

Menopause brain fog refers to cognitive difficulties like memory problems, difficulty concentrating, and mental fatigue that can occur during perimenopause and menopause. Hormone fluctuations are often the culprit.

How long does menopause brain fog typically last?

The duration of brain fog varies, but it can persist for several years during the transition. Managing symptoms and hormone levels can significantly improve cognitive function.

When should I see a doctor about my brain fog?

Consult a doctor if brain fog significantly impacts your daily life or is accompanied by other concerning symptoms like severe headaches or vision changes. They can rule out other potential causes.
