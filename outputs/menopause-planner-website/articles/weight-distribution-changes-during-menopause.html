Common Questions

Why does menopause cause weight distribution changes?
Hormonal fluctuations, particularly a decline in estrogen, lead to increased fat storage around the abdomen. This shift can also be influenced by decreased muscle mass.
What are some effective strategies for managing weight during menopause?
Focus on a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean protein, along with regular exercise including both cardio and strength training. Stress management techniques are also crucial.
Can hormone therapy help with weight changes during menopause?
Hormone therapy may help alleviate some menopause symptoms, but it's not typically prescribed solely for weight management. Consult with your doctor to discuss the potential risks and benefits.
