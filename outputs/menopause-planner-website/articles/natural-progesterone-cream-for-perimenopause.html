Common Questions

How often should I apply progesterone cream?
Typically, it's recommended to apply once or twice daily, following the instructions on the product label. Consult with your doctor for personalized guidance.
Are there any side effects to using natural progesterone cream?
Some women may experience mild side effects like headaches or breast tenderness. These usually subside as your body adjusts; discontinue use and consult your doctor if concerned.
Can I use progesterone cream if I'm still having periods?
Yes, many women use it during perimenopause while still menstruating. Talk to your doctor about the best timing and dosage for your individual needs.
