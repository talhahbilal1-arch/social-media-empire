Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is brain fog during menopause?

Brain fog refers to feelings of confusion, forgetfulness, and difficulty concentrating. It's a common symptom during perimenopause and menopause due to hormonal changes.

Besides tracking, what are some quick ways to improve brain fog?

Prioritize sleep, stay hydrated, engage in regular physical activity, and practice mindfulness. Even small changes can make a big difference in your clarity.

When should I see a doctor about brain fog?

If your brain fog is severe, persistent, or interfering with your daily life, consult your doctor. They can rule out other underlying causes and recommend appropriate treatments.
