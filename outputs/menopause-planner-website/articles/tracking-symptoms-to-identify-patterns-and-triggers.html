Common Questions

Why is it important to track my menopause symptoms?
Tracking helps you identify patterns and triggers, allowing you to make informed decisions about lifestyle changes and treatments that can alleviate your symptoms. You can't manage what you don't measure!
What symptoms should I track?
Focus on tracking hot flashes (time, intensity, triggers), mood changes (alongside daily activities), sleep patterns (duration and quality), and food intake (to spot diet-related triggers). Also note medications, supplements, and dosages.
How long should I track my symptoms before seeing results?
Give it at least a month to start seeing trends. The longer you track, the more accurate your insights will be.
