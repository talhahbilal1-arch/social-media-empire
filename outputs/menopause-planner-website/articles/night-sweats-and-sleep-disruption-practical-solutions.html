Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I experiencing night sweats during menopause?

Hormonal fluctuations, particularly declining estrogen levels, disrupt the body's temperature regulation, leading to night sweats. These fluctuations affect your body's thermostat.

Besides cooling products, what else can I do to reduce night sweats?

Avoid caffeine and alcohol before bed, wear loose-fitting clothing, and maintain a cool room temperature. Consider speaking with your doctor about hormone therapy or other medical options.

How long will my night sweats last?

The duration of night sweats varies greatly among women. Some experience them for a few months, while others may deal with them for several years during perimenopause and beyond.
