Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I do pelvic floor exercises?

Aim for 3 sets of exercises daily for the best results. Consistency is key to strengthening your pelvic floor.

How long does it take to see results from pelvic floor exercises?

Most women notice improvements in bladder control within a few weeks to a few months. Keep practicing for long-term benefits.

Can pelvic floor exercises help with other menopause symptoms?

Yes, strengthening your pelvic floor can also improve sexual function and reduce pelvic pain. A strong core helps overall well-being.
