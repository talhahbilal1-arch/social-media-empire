Frequently Asked Questions

Why are creative hobbies helpful for menopause anxiety?

Creative hobbies provide a distraction from anxious thoughts and promote relaxation. They also boost self-esteem and provide a sense of accomplishment.

What if I'm not 'creative'?

Everyone is creative in their own way! Don't focus on perfection; focus on enjoying the process. There are no wrong answers in creative hobbies.

Even just 15-30 minutes a day can make a difference. Consistency is key, but listen to your body and don't overdo it.
