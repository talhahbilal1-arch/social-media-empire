Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best materials for cooling sheets?

Bamboo, eucalyptus lyocell, and certain microfibers are excellent choices due to their breathability and moisture-wicking properties. Avoid cotton as it tends to trap heat.

How often should I wash my cooling sheets?

Wash your sheets weekly in cool water with a gentle detergent. Avoid using fabric softener, as it can reduce their cooling properties.

Can cooling sheets really help with night sweats?

Yes, cooling sheets can significantly reduce night sweats by wicking away moisture and regulating body temperature. They create a more comfortable sleep environment.
