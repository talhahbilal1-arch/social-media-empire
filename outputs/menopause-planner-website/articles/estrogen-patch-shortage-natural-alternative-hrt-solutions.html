Frequently Asked Questions

What are the risks of not treating menopause symptoms?

Untreated symptoms can significantly impact your quality of life, leading to sleep disturbances, mood changes, and decreased overall well-being. It's essential to find a solution that works for you.

How long does it take for natural remedies to work?

It varies from person to person, but most natural remedies take several weeks to months to show noticeable effects. Consistency is key, and it's important to consult with your doctor to monitor your progress.

Are natural remedies safe for everyone?

While generally safe, natural remedies can interact with certain medications or have side effects. Always consult with your doctor before starting any new treatment, especially if you have underlying health conditions.
