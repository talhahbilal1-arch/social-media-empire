Frequently Asked Questions

Are GLP-1 medications safe for long-term use during menopause?

Long-term safety data is still emerging, so it's essential to discuss risks and benefits with your doctor. Regular monitoring and lifestyle modifications are crucial.

Can GLP-1 medications completely replace lifestyle changes for menopause weight loss?

No, these medications are most effective when combined with a healthy diet and regular exercise. They can aid weight loss, but sustainable results require a holistic approach.

What are some common side effects of GLP-1 medications that I should be aware of?

Common side effects include nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, and constipation. Discuss any concerns with your doctor and report any severe or persistent symptoms immediately.
