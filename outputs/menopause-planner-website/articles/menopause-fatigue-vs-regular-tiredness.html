Frequently Asked Questions

How is menopause fatigue different from regular tiredness?

Menopause fatigue is often persistent, overwhelming, and doesn't improve with rest, unlike typical tiredness. It can significantly impact your ability to function daily.

What are some common causes of menopause fatigue?

Hormonal fluctuations, especially declining estrogen levels, are a primary cause, along with sleep disturbances, mood changes, and increased stress during menopause.

When should I see a doctor about my menopause fatigue?

If your fatigue is severe, persistent, and interferes with your daily life, consult your doctor to rule out other underlying medical conditions and discuss treatment options.
