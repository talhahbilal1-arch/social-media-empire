Common Questions

What are the main alternatives to estrogen patches?
Alternatives include oral estrogen, topical creams or gels, and natural supplements. Discuss these options with your doctor to find the best fit.
How can I manage hot flashes without estrogen?
Lifestyle changes like dressing in layers, avoiding caffeine and alcohol, and practicing relaxation techniques can help. Some women also find relief with black cohosh or evening primrose oil.
Is it safe to switch HRT methods suddenly?
It's crucial to consult with your healthcare provider before making any changes to your HRT regimen. A gradual transition is typically recommended to minimize side effects.
