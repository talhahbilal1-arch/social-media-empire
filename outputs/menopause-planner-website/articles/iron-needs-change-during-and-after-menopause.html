Common Questions

Why do iron needs change during menopause?
The end of menstruation usually lowers your iron needs after menopause. But fluctuating hormones during perimenopause can sometimes affect absorption. Talk to your doctor if you have concerns.
Can I get enough iron from my diet?
A balanced diet rich in iron-rich foods (red meat, leafy greens, fortified cereals) is ideal. However, supplements may be needed if you have low iron levels.
