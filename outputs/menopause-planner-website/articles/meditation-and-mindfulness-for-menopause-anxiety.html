Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I meditate to reduce anxiety?

Aim for at least 10-15 minutes daily. Consistency is key, even short sessions can be effective.

What if I can't clear my mind during meditation?

That's normal! Gently acknowledge your thoughts and guide your focus back to your breath or chosen meditation object. Don't judge yourself.

Are there specific types of meditation for menopause anxiety?

Mindfulness meditation, focusing on the present moment, and loving-kindness meditation, promoting self-compassion, can be particularly helpful.
