Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I see my doctor during menopause?

It depends on your individual symptoms and health history. A good starting point is every 6-12 months, adjusting as needed with your doctor's guidance.

What if my doctor dismisses my menopause symptoms?

Don't be afraid to seek a second opinion from a menopause specialist. Your concerns are valid, and you deserve to be heard and supported.

What are the most important things to discuss with my doctor?

Discuss your specific symptoms, their impact on your quality of life, your family history, and your treatment goals. Be open and honest about your concerns and preferences.
