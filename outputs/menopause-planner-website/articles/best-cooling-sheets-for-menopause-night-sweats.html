Frequently Asked Questions

What makes cooling sheets different from regular sheets?

Cooling sheets are made from materials designed to wick away moisture and promote airflow, helping to regulate body temperature and prevent overheating. This is especially helpful for managing night sweats.

How do I care for cooling sheets?

Most cooling sheets can be machine washed, but it's important to follow the manufacturer's instructions. Avoid using fabric softeners or bleach, as these can damage the fibers and reduce their cooling properties.

Are cooling sheets effective for everyone experiencing night sweats?

While cooling sheets can significantly improve comfort for many, individual results may vary. Factors like room temperature, bedding, and personal physiology can all play a role.
