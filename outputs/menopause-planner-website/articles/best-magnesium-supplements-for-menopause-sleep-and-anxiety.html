Frequently Asked Questions

How much magnesium should I take during menopause?

The recommended daily intake is around 320mg, but consult your doctor for personalized advice. Start with a lower dose and gradually increase as needed.

What are the side effects of magnesium supplements?

Common side effects include diarrhea or stomach upset, especially with magnesium citrate. Choose magnesium glycinate for better tolerance and start with a low dose.

Can magnesium interact with other medications?

Yes, magnesium can interact with certain medications, such as antibiotics and diuretics. Always check with your doctor before starting any new supplement.
