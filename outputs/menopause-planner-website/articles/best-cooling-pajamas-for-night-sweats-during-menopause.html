Frequently Asked Questions

What fabrics are best for cooling pajamas?

Bamboo, cotton, and moisture-wicking synthetics are excellent choices. They promote airflow and wick away moisture, keeping you cool and dry.

How do cooling pajamas help with night sweats?

Cooling pajamas work by drawing heat and moisture away from your body. This helps regulate your body temperature and reduces the severity of night sweats.

Are cooling pajamas only for menopause?

No, cooling pajamas can benefit anyone who experiences night sweats or overheating while sleeping. They are also great for warmer climates.
