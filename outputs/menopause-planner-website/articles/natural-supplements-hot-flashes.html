Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for menopause supplements to work?

Most natural supplements for hot flashes take 4-8 weeks of consistent daily use before you notice meaningful results. Black cohosh tends to work fastest (4-6 weeks), while red clover and evening primrose oil may take 8-12 weeks for full effect.

Are natural supplements safe to take with HRT?

Some supplements can interact with hormone replacement therapy. Black cohosh and phytoestrogens like red clover may have additive estrogenic effects. Always consult your doctor before combining any supplement with HRT or prescription medications.

Which supplement works best for severe hot flashes?

Clinical studies show black cohosh has the strongest evidence for reducing hot flash frequency and severity. Women with severe hot flashes often see the best results combining black cohosh with lifestyle changes like stress management and avoiding triggers like alcohol and spicy foods.
