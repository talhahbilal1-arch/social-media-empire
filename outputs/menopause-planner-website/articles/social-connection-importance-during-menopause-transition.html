Frequently Asked Questions

Why is social connection so important during menopause?

Social support reduces stress, combats feelings of isolation, and provides a sense of community during a significant life transition. Sharing experiences and gaining advice from others can be incredibly empowering.

What if I don't have a strong social network?

That's okay! Start small by joining online forums, attending local events, or reconnecting with old friends. Even small connections can make a big difference.

How can I initiate conversations about menopause with my friends?

Be open and honest about your experiences. You might be surprised to find that others are going through similar things. Starting with a simple, 'I've been experiencing...' can be a great way to begin.
