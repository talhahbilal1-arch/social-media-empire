FAQ

Are menopause supplements safe with HRT?

Most are safe together, but ask your doctor. Black cohosh and magnesium generally don't interact with hormone replacement therapy. Avoid red clover and other phytoestrogens if you have estrogen-sensitive breast cancer. Always disclose all supplements to your healthcare provider.

How long does it take for supplements to help?

Most menopause supplements take 4-8 weeks to show measurable results. Black cohosh: 6-8 weeks. Magnesium: 2-3 weeks. Omega-3s: 4-6 weeks. Be consistent with dosing and give it time. Some women notice results in 2-3 weeks, others need 12 weeks.

What is the best supplement for hot flashes?

Black cohosh is the most researched, with clinical trials showing 40-50% reduction in hot flash frequency. Magnesium glycinate helps with the anxiety piece. The best approach: black cohosh for hot flashes + magnesium for sleep and anxiety. Individual responses vary, so be patient and track what works for you.

The Takeaway

Start with black cohosh + magnesium glycinate. Add fish oil for mood. Give each supplement 6-8 weeks before evaluating. Most women see 40-60% improvement in hot flashes and sleep within 8 weeks. Track your symptoms — what works for your friend might not work for you.
