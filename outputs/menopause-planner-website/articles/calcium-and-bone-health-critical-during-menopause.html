Frequently Asked Questions

How much calcium do I need after menopause?

Most women need 1200mg of calcium daily after menopause. Talk to your doctor to determine the right amount for you.

What else can I do to strengthen my bones besides taking calcium?

Weight-bearing exercise, adequate vitamin D, and a diet rich in fruits and vegetables are crucial. Limiting alcohol and caffeine intake can also help.
