Frequently Asked Questions

Why does body image change during menopause?

Hormonal shifts can cause weight gain, skin changes, and hair thinning, impacting self-perception. These changes can be challenging to accept.

What's the 'mirror trick' mentioned in the title?

Instead of focusing on perceived flaws, consciously identify and appreciate your strengths and positive attributes. This shifts your perspective and boosts self-esteem.

Are there other ways to improve body image during menopause?

Yes! Regular exercise, a healthy diet, and engaging in activities you enjoy can all contribute to feeling better about yourself. Seeking support from friends, family, or a therapist can also be helpful.
