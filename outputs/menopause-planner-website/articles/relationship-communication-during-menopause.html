Common Questions

Why is communication so important during menopause?
Menopause can bring significant physical and emotional changes, and open communication helps partners understand and support each other through these challenges. This fosters empathy and reduces misunderstandings.
What if my partner doesn't understand what I'm going through?
Educate them! Share articles, books, or even your symptom tracker. Help them understand the physical and emotional changes you're experiencing, and explain how they can best support you.
How can I initiate difficult conversations about menopause with my partner?
Choose a calm and private time to talk. Start by expressing your feelings and needs clearly and calmly, focusing on 'I' statements rather than blaming. Be open to listening to their perspective as well.
