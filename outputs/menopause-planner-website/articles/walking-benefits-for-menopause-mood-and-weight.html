Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I walk to improve my menopause mood?

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity walking most days of the week. Even short 10-minute bursts can make a difference.

What if I don't like walking?

Any form of movement can help! Consider swimming, dancing, yoga, or cycling as alternatives to boost your mood and manage menopause symptoms.

Can walking really help with hot flashes?

While walking may not directly eliminate hot flashes, it can help regulate your body temperature and reduce stress, potentially lessening their frequency and intensity.
