Common Questions

What is 'cortisol face'?
Cortisol face refers to the puffiness and inflammation that can occur due to elevated cortisol levels. It's often more noticeable in the morning.
How does perimenopause affect cortisol levels?
Hormonal fluctuations during perimenopause can disrupt the body's stress response system, leading to increased cortisol production. This can manifest in various symptoms, including facial puffiness.
Besides supplements, what else can help?
Prioritizing sleep, managing stress through mindfulness or yoga, and maintaining a healthy diet are crucial. Limiting caffeine and alcohol intake can also make a difference.
