Common Questions

What is the biggest change in HRT research?
Recent studies with longer follow-up periods show that HRT, when started closer to menopause, carries a lower risk profile than previously thought. The timing of initiation seems crucial for safety.
Are there alternatives to HRT for managing menopause symptoms?
Yes, lifestyle changes, such as diet and exercise, alongside supplements like black cohosh and magnesium glycinate, can help manage symptoms. Talk to your doctor about the best approach for you.
What should I discuss with my doctor before starting HRT?
Discuss your personal and family medical history, your specific symptoms, and your concerns about HRT. Ask about the different types of HRT and their associated risks and benefits.
