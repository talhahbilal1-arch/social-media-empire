Frequently Asked Questions

What foods are considered anti-inflammatory?

Anti-inflammatory foods include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats like those found in olive oil and avocados. Avoid processed foods, sugary drinks, and excessive red meat.

How long does it take to see results from an anti-inflammatory diet?

While individual results may vary, many women report feeling a difference within a few weeks of consistently following an anti-inflammatory eating plan. It's important to be patient and consistent.

Can an anti-inflammatory diet completely eliminate hot flashes?

While an anti-inflammatory diet can significantly reduce the frequency and severity of hot flashes, it may not eliminate them entirely. It's best used as part of a holistic approach that includes other lifestyle changes.
