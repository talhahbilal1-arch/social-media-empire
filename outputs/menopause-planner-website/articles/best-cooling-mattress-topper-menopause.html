Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a cooling mattress topper during menopause?

Night sweats are a common symptom of menopause, and a cooling mattress topper can help regulate your body temperature and wick away moisture, leading to more restful sleep.

What materials are best for cooling mattress toppers?

Gel-infused memory foam, bamboo, and moisture-wicking fabrics are all excellent choices for cooling mattress toppers. They help dissipate heat and keep you dry.

How do I care for my cooling mattress topper?

Always follow the manufacturer's instructions, but generally, you can spot clean spills and wash the topper regularly to maintain its cleanliness and effectiveness.
