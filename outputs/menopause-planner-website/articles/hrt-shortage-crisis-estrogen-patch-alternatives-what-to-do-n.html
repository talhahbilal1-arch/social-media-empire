Common Questions

What are some natural alternatives to estrogen patches?
Lifestyle changes like diet, exercise, and stress management can help. Certain herbal remedies may also provide relief, but consult your doctor first.
How can I cope with the anxiety of the HRT shortage?
Focus on what you can control: healthy habits, symptom tracking, and exploring alternative therapies. Support groups can also provide valuable emotional support.
When should I see a doctor about menopause symptoms?
Consult your doctor if your symptoms are severe, interfering with your daily life, or if you have concerns about the HRT shortage. They can help you explore all available options.
