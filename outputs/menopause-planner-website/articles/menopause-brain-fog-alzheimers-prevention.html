Common Questions

What are the early warning signs of Alzheimer's?
Difficulty remembering newly learned information, asking the same questions repeatedly, and getting lost in familiar places can be early indicators. Consulting a doctor is crucial for accurate diagnosis.
How can I improve my memory during menopause?
Prioritize sleep, manage stress through techniques like meditation, and engage in regular physical activity to boost blood flow to the brain. A healthy diet rich in antioxidants is also beneficial.
Is brain fog a normal part of menopause?
Yes, brain fog is a common symptom during perimenopause and menopause due to hormonal fluctuations, particularly estrogen. While frustrating, it's usually temporary and manageable.
