Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I feel so lonely during menopause?

Hormonal changes can affect mood and social interactions, leading to feelings of isolation. Remember, you're not alone, and many women experience this.

How can I build stronger connections with others?

Initiate conversations, join groups with shared interests, and prioritize quality time with loved ones. Even small steps can make a big difference.

What if I don't have close friends or family nearby?

Explore online communities, volunteer in your local area, or consider joining a support group. Focus on building new connections that resonate with you.
