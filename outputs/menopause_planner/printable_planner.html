How to Use This Planner

This planner is designed to help you track, understand, and manage your menopause journey. Here is what each section is for.

My Menopause Stage

Identify where you are in your journey. This helps you and your healthcare team understand your baseline.

My Healthcare Team

Keep all your provider contacts in one place so you always have them when you need them.

Symptom Overview Checklist

Review the comprehensive list of menopause symptoms. Check the ones you experience to get a clear picture of your symptoms.

Monthly Symptom Trackers

Use these daily grids to log hot flashes, night sweats, sleep quality, mood, energy, and exercise. Over time, you will see patterns that help you and your doctor make informed decisions.

Weekly Hot Flash Logs

For detailed hot flash tracking. Record time, duration, intensity, triggers, and what helped. Great for identifying your personal triggers.

Sleep Quality Tracker

Track bedtime, wake time, night sweats, and overall sleep quality. Sleep is one of the most affected areas during menopause.

Mood & Energy Log

Monitor mood fluctuations throughout the day and identify triggers and coping strategies that work for you.

Medication & Supplement Tracker

Keep a running list of everything you take, including dosage, timing, purpose, and any side effects.

Doctor Visit Preparation

Prepare for appointments with a checklist, questions, and a symptom summary you can share with your provider.

Self-Care Ideas

Browse 40+ ideas organized by category. Circle or highlight the ones that resonate with you.

Tip: You do not need to fill in every section every day. Start with what feels manageable and build from there. Even tracking a few things consistently can reveal valuable patterns.
