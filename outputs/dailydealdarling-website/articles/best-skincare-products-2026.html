Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to determine my skin type?

Observe your skin throughout the day. Oily skin is shiny, dry skin feels tight, and combination skin has both.

How often should I exfoliate my skin?

Generally, 1-3 times per week is sufficient. Over-exfoliating can damage the skin barrier.

Why is sunscreen so important?

Sunscreen protects your skin from harmful UV rays, preventing premature aging and reducing the risk of skin cancer. It's a daily essential.
