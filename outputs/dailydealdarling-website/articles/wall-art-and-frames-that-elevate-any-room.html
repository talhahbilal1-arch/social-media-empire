Frequently Asked Questions

What frame size is best for a small room?

Opt for smaller frame sizes or create a gallery wall with varying sizes to avoid overwhelming the space. Consider the scale of the room when choosing your art and frame sizes.

How can I arrange frames to make a room look bigger?

Hang frames at eye level and leave ample space between them to create a sense of openness. Grouping frames in a gallery wall can also visually expand the wall space.

What color frame is best for a small space?

Lighter colored frames, like white, light wood, or acrylic, reflect light and make the room feel brighter and more spacious. Darker frames can work if used sparingly and strategically.
