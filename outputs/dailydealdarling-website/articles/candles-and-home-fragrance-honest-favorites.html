Common Questions

How do I make my candles last longer?
Always trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each burn. Avoid drafts and let the candle burn long enough for the entire surface to melt to prevent tunneling.
Why is my candle tunneling?
Tunneling happens when a candle doesn't burn long enough to melt the wax evenly across the top. Ensure the first burn is long enough to create a full melt pool.
What are the best candle scents for relaxation?
Lavender, chamomile, and sandalwood are known for their calming properties. Consider these for creating a relaxing atmosphere in your home.
