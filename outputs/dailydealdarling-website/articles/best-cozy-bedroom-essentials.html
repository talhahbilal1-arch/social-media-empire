FAQ

How can I make my bedroom cozier on a budget?

Texture, warmth, and lighting. A good throw blanket ($25-40), soft pillows, and fairy lights ($15-25) transform a basic bedroom. The psychology is real — textures and warm lighting make spaces feel more inviting. Add a white noise machine for better sleep.

What thread count is best for sheets?

300-600 thread count is ideal. Anything above 1000 is marketing hype. The material quality matters more — Egyptian cotton and bamboo are the best. Focus on feel, not just numbers.

The Takeaway

A cozy bedroom doesn't require a budget remodel. Start with a quality throw blanket, upgrade your pillowcase, add warm lighting, and use white noise. These five items under $150 create a bedroom you'll actually want to spend time in.
