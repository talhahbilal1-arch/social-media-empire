Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right size storage bins?

Measure the space where you plan to store them and consider the items you'll be storing. Opt for a variety of sizes to maximize space.

What's the best way to declutter before organizing?

Start by sorting items into categories: keep, donate, or discard. Be honest with yourself about what you truly need and use.

How often should I declutter and reorganize?

Aim for a major decluttering session at least twice a year, such as during spring cleaning. Regular maintenance, like putting things back in their place, is also essential.
