Frequently Asked Questions

Can portable blenders actually crush ice?

Only the more powerful models can. Look for portable blenders with at least 200 watts of motor power and stainless steel blades with 6 points. Budget models under 100 watts will spin ice around without crushing it. Our top pick, the BlendJet 2, handles small ice cubes and frozen fruit reliably.

How long does a portable blender battery last?

Most quality portable blenders get 12-15 full blends per charge via USB-C. That means roughly one smoothie per day for two weeks before needing a charge. Charging takes 2-3 hours for a full battery. Avoid models that only offer 6-8 blends per charge — you will be constantly recharging.

Are portable blenders worth it or should I just buy a regular blender?

If you already have counter space for a full-size blender and only blend at home, a regular blender is more powerful. But portable blenders shine for travel, offices, dorms, and small kitchens. They are also easier to clean — most are self-cleaning with water and a drop of soap. For people who want smoothies at work or the gym, a portable blender is absolutely worth $25-40.
