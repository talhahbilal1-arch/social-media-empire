Frequently Asked Questions

Is a manual chopper better than an electric food processor?

It depends on your needs! Manual choppers are best for small tasks and easy cleanup, while food processors are better for larger quantities and more complex tasks.

How do I clean a manual food chopper?

Most manual choppers are easy to disassemble and clean. Simply rinse the blades and container with soap and water, or place them in the dishwasher if they are dishwasher-safe.
