Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my hair tools?

Ideally, you should clean your hair tools after each use to remove product buildup and debris. At minimum, aim to clean them weekly.

Can heat protectant really prevent damage?

Yes! Heat protectant forms a barrier between your hair and the hot tool, minimizing damage from heat exposure and keeping your hair healthy.

What temperature should I use on my hair tools?

Start with a lower temperature setting and gradually increase it until you achieve the desired results. Finer hair requires lower heat than thicker hair.
