Common Questions

How do I keep my throw pillows fluffy?
Fluff your pillows regularly and consider using high-quality inserts that retain their shape. Washing them according to the care instructions also helps.
What's the best way to wash a sherpa fleece blanket?
Machine wash on a gentle cycle with cold water and tumble dry on low heat. Avoid using bleach or fabric softener to maintain its softness.
Are faux fur blankets warm?
Yes, faux fur blankets are generally warm and cozy, making them perfect for snuggling up on the couch during colder months.
