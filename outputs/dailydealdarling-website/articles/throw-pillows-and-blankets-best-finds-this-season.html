FAQ

How do I choose pillows and blankets for spring?

Look for lighter materials like cotton or linen blends that are breathable. Opt for washable covers to keep them fresh, and consider pastel or muted tones that reflect the season.

What's the best way to mix patterns with pillows and blankets?

Try pairing a large, dominant pattern with smaller, simpler patterns. Stick to a similar color palette to tie them all together and avoid overwhelming the space.
