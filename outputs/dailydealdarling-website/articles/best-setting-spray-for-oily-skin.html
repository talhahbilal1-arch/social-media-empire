Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply setting spray for oily skin?

Hold the bottle 8-10 inches from your face and spray in an 'X' and 'T' formation. Let it dry completely to set your makeup and control oil.

Can setting spray actually help with oily skin?

Yes! Setting sprays formulated for oily skin often contain ingredients that absorb excess oil and create a matte finish, helping to keep shine at bay.

Is setting spray bad for my skin?

Not usually, but choose non-comedogenic formulas to prevent clogged pores. Always remove your makeup at the end of the day and follow with a good skincare routine.
