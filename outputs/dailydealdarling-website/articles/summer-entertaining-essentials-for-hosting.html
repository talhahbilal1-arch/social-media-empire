FAQ

What's the best way to serve appetizers outdoors?

Using tiered stands or platters helps maximize space and creates an appealing display. Labeling items can also be helpful, especially if you have dietary considerations.

How can I keep drinks cold without a big cooler?

Consider using stylish tubs or buckets filled with ice for different types of beverages. You can also pre-chill drinks indoors and transfer them to insulated carriers right before guests arrive.
