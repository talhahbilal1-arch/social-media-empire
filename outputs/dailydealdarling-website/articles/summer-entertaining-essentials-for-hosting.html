Common Questions

How can I keep bugs away from my food during outdoor gatherings?
Use mesh food covers or citronella candles to deter insects. Also, avoid leaving food uncovered for extended periods.
What's the best way to clean up after a summer party?
Enlist help from your guests! Have designated trash and recycling bins, and consider using disposable plates and cutlery for easier cleanup.
How do I keep my drinks cold outside without a refrigerator?
Invest in insulated beverage tubs or coolers filled with ice. Consider adding frozen water bottles to keep the ice from melting too quickly.
