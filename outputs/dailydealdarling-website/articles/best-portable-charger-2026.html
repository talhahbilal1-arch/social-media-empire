Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right portable charger capacity?

Consider how many times you need to charge your devices. A 10000mAh charger typically charges a phone 2-3 times, while a 20000mAh charger can charge it 4-6 times.

What is fast charging, and is it important?

Fast charging allows you to charge your devices more quickly. Look for chargers that support Power Delivery (PD) or Quick Charge (QC) for faster charging speeds.

Are portable chargers safe to use?

Yes, reputable portable chargers have built-in safety features to prevent overcharging, overheating, and short circuits. Always purchase from trusted brands.

Can I bring a portable charger on a plane?

Yes, you can usually bring portable chargers on a plane in your carry-on luggage. However, there may be restrictions on the capacity, so check with your airline before traveling.
