The best part? Most marble boards run $7-15 depending on size. They're cold to the touch, which is actually perfect for rolling out pie dough or keeping butter from melting during summer baking. Mine lives on my counter 24/7 now, and guests think it's this carefully curated design choice. (It is, but they don't need to know it cost less than takeout.)
