Stackable Cosmetic Holders for Maximum Flexibility

Another brilliant solution for tiny bathroom counters is stackable cosmetic holders. These modular organizers let you build your storage system piece by piece, adding or removing levels based on what you need.

I started with just two stackable drawers for my eyeshadow palettes and lipsticks, then added a third tier when my collection grew (those beauty sales get me every time). The beauty of stackable organizers is that they grow with you, and you only pay for what you actually need.

These holders work especially well if you have different types of products that need different storage solutions. Smart Organization Tips for Tiny Bathrooms

Beyond choosing the right organizers, there are some strategy tricks that'll help you maximize your small bathroom counter:

Declutter ruthlessly. Be honest about what you actually use. If you haven't touched that eyeshadow palette in six months, it's time to donate it or toss it if it's expired.

Use drawer organizers too. Your under-sink cabinet or bathroom drawers can hold backup products and things you don't use daily. Keep only your everyday essentials on the counter.

Go vertical whenever possible. Wall-mounted shelves, over-the-toilet storage, and magnetic strips for bobby pins and tweezers free up counter space.

Group products by routine. Keep your morning routine products together and your nighttime skincare separate. This makes getting ready so much faster.

Clean as you go. Wipe down your organizers weekly to prevent makeup dust buildup. It takes two minutes and keeps everything looking fresh.
