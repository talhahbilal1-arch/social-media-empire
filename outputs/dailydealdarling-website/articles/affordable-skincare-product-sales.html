Smart Shopping Strategies for Budget Skincare:

1. Focus on Core Ingredients: You don't need a complex 10-step routine filled with exotic ingredients to see results. Prioritize products with proven, powerhouse ingredients like hyaluronic acid for hydration, niacinamide for pore refinement and brightening, vitamin C for antioxidant protection and glow, and salicylic acid for acne-prone skin. These ingredients are widely available in affordable formulations.

For example, a tinted moisturizer with SPF offers hydration, sun protection, and a touch of coverage, saving you from buying separate items. Similarly, a gentle cleanser can often remove light makeup, simplifying your routine and your shopping list.
