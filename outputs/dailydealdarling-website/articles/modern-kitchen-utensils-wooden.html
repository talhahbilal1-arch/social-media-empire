FAQ

How do you care for modern wooden kitchen utensils?

To keep your wooden utensils looking great and lasting for years, always hand wash them with mild soap and warm water, then dry immediately. Avoid the dishwasher, as high heat can warp and crack the wood. For extra protection, you can periodically rub them with food-safe mineral oil or beeswax to prevent drying and enhance their natural beauty.
