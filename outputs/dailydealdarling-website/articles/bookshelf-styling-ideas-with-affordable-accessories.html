Frequently Asked Questions

How do I avoid a cluttered look on my bookshelf?

Embrace negative space! Leave some areas intentionally empty to create visual breathing room and prevent overwhelm.

What's the best way to arrange books on a shelf?

Mix it up! Combine vertical stacks with horizontal piles to add visual interest and create varying heights.

How do I incorporate personal items without making it look messy?

Choose a few meaningful pieces and display them intentionally. Group similar items together for a cohesive look.
