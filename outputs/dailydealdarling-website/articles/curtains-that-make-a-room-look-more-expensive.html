FAQ

How high should I hang my curtains to make my room look bigger?

Hang your curtain rod as close to the ceiling as possible to create the illusion of height. Ideally, the bottom of your curtains should just barely touch the floor.

What's the best way to iron or steam curtains?

Use a garment steamer to remove wrinkles while the curtains are hanging. If you prefer ironing, use a low heat setting and a pressing cloth to protect the fabric.
