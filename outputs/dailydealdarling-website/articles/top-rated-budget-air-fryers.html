Frequently Asked Questions

What size air fryer is best for one or two people?

A 2-3 quart air fryer is generally sufficient for singles or couples. It's compact and won't take up too much counter space.

Are air fryers really healthy?

Air fryers use significantly less oil than traditional frying, making them a healthier option. However, it's still important to choose healthy ingredients.

How do I clean my air fryer?

Most air fryer baskets are dishwasher-safe, but always check the manufacturer's instructions. For stubborn food residue, soak the basket in warm, soapy water before washing.
