FAQ

How do I keep my laundry room smelling fresh?

I like to use a diffuser with essential oils or scented sachets in my drawers. Just be sure the scent isn't too overpowering, or it could transfer to your clothes.

What's the best way to store dryer sheets?

I keep mine in a small, airtight container to prevent them from drying out. You can also find cute little dispensers specifically designed for dryer sheets.
