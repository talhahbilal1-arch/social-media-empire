FAQ

How do I declutter my laundry room?

Start by taking everything out and sorting it into categories: keep, donate, or trash. Be honest with yourself about what you actually use and get rid of the rest!

What's the best way to organize laundry supplies?

Use baskets, bins, or shelves to keep everything contained and easy to find. Labeling everything can also help you stay organized in the long run.
