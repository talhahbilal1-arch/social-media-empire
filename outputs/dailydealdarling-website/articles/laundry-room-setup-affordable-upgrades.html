FAQ

How do I maximize space in a small laundry room?

Think vertical! Floating shelves, wall-mounted organizers, and even over-the-door organizers can make the most of limited space. Also, declutter ruthlessly!

What are some affordable ways to make a laundry room more inviting?

Adding a small rug, some plants (real or fake), and brighter lighting can instantly make the space feel more cheerful. You can also swap out plastic detergent bottles for nicer-looking glass containers.
