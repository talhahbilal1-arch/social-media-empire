Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prevent my plastic containers from staining?

Avoid heating tomato-based sauces in plastic containers. Rinse them immediately after use and try a baking soda paste for stubborn stains.

Are glass containers really safe to put in the oven?

Yes, as long as they are labeled as oven-safe! Be sure to avoid extreme temperature changes to prevent shattering. Always remove the lid before putting them in the oven.

How should I organize my meal prep containers for optimal space?

Stackable containers are key! Store lids separately in a container or lid organizer to maximize space and keep everything tidy.
