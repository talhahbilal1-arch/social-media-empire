FAQ

Are cheaper meal prep containers worth it?

Sometimes, but I've found they often warp, melt, or develop leaky seals quickly. Investing a little more upfront usually saves money and frustration long-term.

Can you put glass containers in the freezer?

Yes, most high-quality glass containers like the Anchor Hocking ones are freezer-safe. Just be sure to leave a little headspace for food expansion as it freezes.
