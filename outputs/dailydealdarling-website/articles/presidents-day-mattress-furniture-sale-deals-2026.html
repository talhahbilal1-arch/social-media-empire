Frequently Asked Questions

How can I tell if a Presidents Day furniture sale is legitimate?

Compare prices across multiple retailers and check the product's review history. Look for significant discounts compared to its usual price, not just inflated prices before the sale.

What are some common tricks retailers use during furniture sales?

They may inflate the original price, offer small discounts on low-quality items, or advertise 'clearance' items that are actually discontinued or outdated models.

When is the best time to shop for furniture during Presidents Day weekend?

Often, the best deals are available early in the sale, but keep checking throughout the weekend as retailers may add new promotions or adjust prices.
