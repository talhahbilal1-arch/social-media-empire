Frequently Asked Questions

What size accessories do I need for my air fryer?

Measure the inside of your air fryer basket or consult the manufacturer's instructions. Generally, 8-inch accessories fit most standard air fryers.

How do I clean my air fryer accessories?

Most air fryer accessories are dishwasher safe, but always check the manufacturer's instructions. For best results, hand wash with warm soapy water to prolong their lifespan.

Are silicone air fryer liners safe?

Yes, as long as they are made from food-grade silicone and are heat-resistant to the temperatures you are using in your air fryer. Always check the product specifications.
