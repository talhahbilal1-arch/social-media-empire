FAQ

What are the best fabrics for comfortable loungewear?

Look for soft, breathable fabrics like cotton, fleece, or rayon blends. These materials will keep you comfortable without making you overheat.

How do I care for my loungewear to make it last longer?

Always follow the care instructions on the label. Washing your loungewear inside out and avoiding high heat in the dryer can help prevent pilling and fading.
