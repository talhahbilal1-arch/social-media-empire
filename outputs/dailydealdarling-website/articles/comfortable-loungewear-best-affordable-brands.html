Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my loungewear?

Wash your loungewear every 2-3 wears to maintain freshness and prevent the buildup of bacteria. Always follow the care instructions on the label for best results.

What's the best way to prevent pilling on fleece loungewear?

Turn your fleece loungewear inside out before washing and use a gentle cycle with cold water. Avoid using fabric softener, as it can contribute to pilling.
