Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make my home feel cozier for fall on a budget?

Focus on small swaps like throw blankets, scented candles, and cozy lighting. Layering textures and adding warm colors can also make a big difference.

What are some affordable ways to add fall scents to my home?

Besides candles, consider using essential oil diffusers with fall-inspired scents like cinnamon, clove, and pumpkin spice. Simmering spices on the stovetop is another budget-friendly option.

How often should I wash my throw blankets?

Wash your throw blankets every 2-3 weeks, or more often if they are frequently used or exposed to pets. Always follow the care instructions on the label.
