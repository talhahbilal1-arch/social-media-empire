Frequently Asked Questions

Is a $100 air fryer worth it compared to a $200 model?

Absolutely. Budget air fryers in 2026 perform remarkably well for everyday cooking. You lose some premium features like smart WiFi connectivity and dual-zone cooking, but the core air frying performance is nearly identical to pricier models.

What size air fryer should I get for a family of four?

For a family of four, aim for at least a 5-quart capacity. This lets you cook a full batch of fries, wings, or vegetables without overcrowding, which is key for crispy results.

How much electricity does an air fryer use compared to a regular oven?

Air fryers use roughly 1,200-1,500 watts and cook food 20-30% faster than conventional ovens. Over a year, most households save $50-80 on energy bills by using an air fryer for smaller meals instead of heating a full oven.
