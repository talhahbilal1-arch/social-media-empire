Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose the right under cabinet lighting?

Consider the size of your space, desired brightness, and whether you prefer a wired or wireless solution. Rechargeable options offer the most flexibility!

How do I install under cabinet lighting?

Most wireless options are peel-and-stick, making installation a breeze. Wired options may require professional installation, but rechargeable are great for DIY.

What's the best color temperature for under cabinet lighting?

A warm white (3000K) is often recommended for a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Cool white (4000K) provides brighter, more focused light.
