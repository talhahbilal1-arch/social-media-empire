Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ideal water temperature for brewing coffee?

The ideal brewing temperature is between 195-205°F (90-96°C). This range extracts the most flavor without burning the coffee.

How often should I clean my coffee maker?

Ideally, you should clean your coffee maker every month to prevent mineral buildup and ensure optimal performance. Use a descaling solution or a mixture of water and vinegar.

What type of coffee grounds should I use?

Use a medium grind for drip coffee makers. This allows for optimal extraction without over-extraction or under-extraction, leading to a balanced flavor.
