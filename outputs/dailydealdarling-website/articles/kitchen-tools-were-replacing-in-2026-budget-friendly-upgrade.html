Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace my kitchen tools?

Replace tools showing signs of wear, like dull knives or melting plastic. Quality tools should last several years.

Are silicone utensils safe to use at high temperatures?

Yes, most silicone utensils are heat-resistant up to 400-500°F (200-260°C). Always check the manufacturer's instructions.

How do I properly care for my new chef's knife?

Hand-wash your knife with mild soap and water, and dry it immediately. Avoid using it on hard surfaces like glass or ceramic.
