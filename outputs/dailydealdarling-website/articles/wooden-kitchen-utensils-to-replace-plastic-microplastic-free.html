Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean wooden kitchen utensils?

Wash them by hand with warm soapy water and avoid soaking them for extended periods. Let them air dry completely to prevent cracking.

How often should I oil my wooden utensils?

Oiling your utensils every few weeks or when they start to look dry will help them last longer. Use food-grade mineral oil or beeswax.

Are wooden utensils safe to use with non-stick cookware?

Yes, wooden utensils are generally safe for non-stick cookware as they are less likely to scratch the surface compared to metal utensils.
