Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my kids to actually use these organizers?

Make it a game! Turn cleanup into a fun activity with rewards or incentives. Also, label everything clearly with pictures.

What's the best way to declutter before organizing?

Start by going through everything with your child and donating or discarding items they no longer use or love. Be ruthless!

How often should I reorganize?

A quick weekly tidy is helpful, but a more thorough reorganization should be done every 3-6 months to reassess needs and declutter further.
