FAQ

How do I organize a small bathroom?

Think vertical. Use over-door organizers, under-sink shelving, and wall-mounted storage. Clear bins help you see what you have. Group items by category — skincare, medications, hair tools — and label everything. Remove anything unused in 3 months.

What goes under the sink for organization?

Cleaning supplies, extra toiletries, and medications. Use drawer organizers or stackable bins to maximize awkward plumbing space. Keep a small trash bin for expired products. Label everything so you know what you're storing.

The Takeaway

A clutter-free bathroom takes one afternoon and costs under $100. Start with under-sink bins, add drawer dividers, and label everything. The result? You'll actually enjoy your bathroom, and you'll know exactly where everything is.
