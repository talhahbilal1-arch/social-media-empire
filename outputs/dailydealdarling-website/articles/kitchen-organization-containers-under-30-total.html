Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to clean food storage containers?

Most containers are dishwasher safe, but hand washing with warm, soapy water is recommended for longevity. Avoid abrasive sponges that can scratch the plastic.

How do I prevent pantry moths?

Store dry goods in airtight containers and regularly check for signs of infestation. Bay leaves placed in the pantry can also help deter moths.

What size containers should I buy?

Consider the types of food you typically store. Opt for a variety of sizes to accommodate everything from spices to flour and cereal.
