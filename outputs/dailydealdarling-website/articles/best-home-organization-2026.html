Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I declutter my home?

Aim for a major decluttering session at least twice a year, and a quick tidy-up weekly. Regular maintenance prevents overwhelming build-up.

What's the best way to start organizing a cluttered space?

Start small by focusing on one area at a time, like a drawer or shelf. Sort items into keep, donate, and discard piles for a manageable approach.

Where can I donate unwanted items?

Consider local charities, thrift stores, or organizations that support specific causes. Many offer pick-up services for larger items, making it even easier.
