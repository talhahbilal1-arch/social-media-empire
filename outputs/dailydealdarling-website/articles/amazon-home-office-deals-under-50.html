Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most important home office upgrade under $50?

A monitor riser or laptop stand. Raising your screen to eye level eliminates the neck strain that causes headaches and fatigue during long work sessions. This single $25-35 upgrade has the biggest impact on comfort and productivity according to ergonomic research.

Is a desk lamp really necessary if I have overhead lighting?

Yes. Overhead lighting creates shadows and glare on your screen that cause eye strain over time. A dedicated desk lamp with adjustable color temperature (warm for relaxation, cool for focus) reduces eye fatigue significantly. LED desk lamps use minimal electricity and last 50,000+ hours.

How can I organize a small home office desk?

The key is vertical organization. Use a monitor stand with built-in storage underneath for your keyboard. Add a small desk organizer for pens and supplies. Route cables with adhesive cable clips. Most people can transform a cluttered desk into a clean workspace with $30-40 in organizers.
