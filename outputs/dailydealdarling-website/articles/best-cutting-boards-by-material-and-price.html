FAQ

Which cutting board material is the most hygienic?

Plastic and glass are generally considered the most hygienic because they're non-porous and easy to disinfect. However, proper cleaning and maintenance are key for any material.

How often should I oil my wooden or bamboo cutting board?

You should oil your wooden or bamboo cutting board every few weeks, or whenever it starts to look dry. Food-grade mineral oil or beeswax are good options.
