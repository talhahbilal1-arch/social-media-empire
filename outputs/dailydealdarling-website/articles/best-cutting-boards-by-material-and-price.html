Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best material for a cutting board?

Maple is generally considered the best due to its durability, knife-friendliness, and antibacterial properties. Bamboo is a great sustainable alternative.

How do I clean and maintain my cutting board?

Wash wood and bamboo cutting boards with warm, soapy water and dry them thoroughly. Oiling wood boards regularly helps prevent cracking.

Are plastic cutting boards safe?

Yes, plastic cutting boards are safe as long as you clean them thoroughly after each use. Replace them when they become heavily scratched.
