Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a curling iron and a curling wand?

A curling iron has a clamp to hold the hair in place, while a curling wand requires you to wrap the hair around the barrel manually. Wands tend to create more natural-looking waves.

How do I choose the right barrel size for my hair?

Smaller barrels (around 1 inch) are best for tight curls, while larger barrels (1.25 inches or more) are ideal for loose waves. Consider your hair length and desired look.

How can I protect my hair from heat damage?

Always use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tool. Also, use the lowest heat setting possible that still achieves your desired results.
