Frequently Asked Questions

How can I build an affordable skincare routine?

Focus on essential steps like cleansing, moisturizing, and sun protection. Choose budget-friendly brands with proven ingredients.

What are the most important skincare ingredients to look for?

Hyaluronic acid for hydration, niacinamide for blemishes, and retinol for anti-aging are great starting points. Always patch-test new products!

How often should I exfoliate?

Exfoliate 1-2 times per week to remove dead skin cells and reveal a brighter complexion. Over-exfoliating can cause irritation, so listen to your skin.
