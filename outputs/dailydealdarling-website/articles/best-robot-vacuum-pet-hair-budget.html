Common Questions

Can a budget robot vacuum really handle heavy pet hair shedding?
Yes, but you need to look for specific features: rubber extractors instead of bristle brushes (hair wraps less), strong suction above 2000Pa, and a self-emptying base helps enormously. Our top pick handles homes with 2+ pets on a daily cleaning schedule without clogging.
How often should I run a robot vacuum if I have pets?
For homes with pets, daily runs are ideal. Most modern robot vacuums can be scheduled to clean while you are at work. Running daily prevents hair buildup and keeps allergens low. For heavy shedders like Huskies or Golden Retrievers, twice daily is even better.
Do robot vacuums scare cats and dogs?
Most pets adjust within a week. Start by running the vacuum while you are home so your pet associates the sound with normalcy. The quieter models we recommend (under 65dB) are less likely to spook anxious pets. Some dogs even learn to ride them.
