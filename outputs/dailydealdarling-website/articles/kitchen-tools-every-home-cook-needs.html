Common Questions

How often should I sharpen my chef's knife?
Sharpening every few weeks is ideal for maintaining a sharp edge. A honing steel can be used more frequently to realign the blade.
Are glass mixing bowls better than stainless steel?
Glass bowls are non-reactive and allow you to see what's inside, while stainless steel is more durable and lightweight. It depends on preference!
What is the best way to clean my peeler?
Most peelers are dishwasher safe, but hand-washing is often recommended to prolong their lifespan. Use warm, soapy water and a soft sponge.
