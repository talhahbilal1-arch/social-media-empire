Frequently Asked Questions

How do I maintain an organized pantry?

Regularly declutter by checking expiration dates and donating unwanted items. Implement a 'first in, first out' system to use older items first.

What's the best way to store open packages in the pantry?

Transfer the contents to airtight containers to prevent staleness and pest infestations. Label the containers with the date they were opened.

How often should I clean my pantry?

Wipe down shelves and surfaces at least once a month to prevent spills and crumbs from accumulating. A deeper clean should happen seasonally.
