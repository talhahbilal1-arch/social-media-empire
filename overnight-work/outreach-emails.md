## GENERAL OUTREACH BEST PRACTICES

**Timing:**
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM EST (peak email checking)
- Avoid Monday mornings (inbox overload) and Friday afternoons (checked out)

**Follow-up cadence:**
- Day 0: Initial email
- Day 3: If no reply, send brief follow-up ("Just bumping this up — any interest?")
- Day 7: Final follow-up ("Last note on this — happy to connect whenever timing works")
- After 3 touches with no reply, move on

**Subject line rules:**
- Under 50 characters
- No ALL CAPS or exclamation marks
- Personalize when possible (use their name, company, or tool name)
- Lead with value or curiosity, not a sales pitch

**Body rules:**
- Under 150 words (respect their time)
- One clear ask (not three)
- Specific about what you offer
- End with a question (increases reply rate)
- No attachments on first email (triggers spam filters)

**Tracking:**
- Use a simple spreadsheet: Name, Email, Date Sent, Follow-up Date, Status
- Track: Sent, Opened (if using email tracking), Replied, Meeting Booked, Converted
- Goal: 30%+ reply rate, 10%+ meeting rate
