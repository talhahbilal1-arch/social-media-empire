## GUMROAD DISCOVER STRATEGY

### Should You Enable Discover? YES.

**Pros:**
- Free organic traffic (no ad cost)
- Algorithm rewards quality products with good ratings and sales velocity
- Pre-qualified audience (people browsing are ready to buy)
- Cumulative — once products get initial sales, algorithm feeds more visibility
- No downsides — products can still be promoted externally AND appear in Discover

**Cons/Challenges:**
- Needs $10+ in sales and solid ratings before featuring
- High-quality descriptions and images required (but worth doing anyway)
- Competition in similar categories
- Exact ranking factors aren't public

**Action Items:**
1. Implement all optimization recommendations above (titles, descriptions, images, tags)
2. Invest in professional Canva covers ($50-150 or DIY)
3. Get first sales fast via email, social, direct outreach (triggers Discover eligibility)
4. Use tier pricing — offer $17-27 entry point to accelerate first sales
5. Consider 30% affiliate commission for first 30 days to boost visibility

### Recommended Discover Categories:
- "Business & Money" > "Courses & Training" (all coaching products)
- "Health & Wellness" > "Fitness" (AI Fitness Coach Vault)
- "Business & Money" > "Marketing & Sales" (Pinterest Blueprint)
