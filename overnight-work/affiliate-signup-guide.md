## Key Lessons for Your Situation

1. **Start with existing strength:** Optimize Amazon Associates first. You already have tags active and understand your audience.

2. **High-commission digital products:** ClickBank is purpose-built for your Gumroad/PromptBase products' price point. Lean into this.

3. **AI tools are hot:** Your pilottools.ai has massive potential with Semrush, ElevenLabs, Surfer, Copy.ai, Writesonic. Create content comparing tools and features.

4. **Recurring revenue matters:** Writesonic (30%), Copy.ai (45% Y1), Grammarly ($20/conversion) create sustainable income.

5. **Don't chase low-value programs:** 3-7% commissions (Rakuten, lower-tier CJ) require massive volume. Focus on 15%+ programs.

6. **Cookie duration is critical:** Longer cookies (120 days Semrush, 90 days Grammarly/Surfer) mean better conversion tracking and more sales attributed to you.

7. **Approval is easy for most:** You'll be approved within days for almost all programs. The hard part is traffic and conversions.
