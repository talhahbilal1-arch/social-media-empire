## What Could Go Wrong (Reality Check)

### Mistake 1: Treating Everything as Equal Priority
Freelance + coaching will generate 70-80% of revenue through Month 6. Don't spread thin across 7 streams.

### Mistake 2: Expecting Viral Growth
15 pins/day for 6 months = 2,700 pins. Only 2-5% will drive meaningful traffic. Expect hockey-stick growth starting Month 4-6, not Month 1.

### Mistake 3: Underpricing Freelance Work
Start at $50-60/hour. Low pricing attracts low-quality clients with scope creep and late payments.

### Mistake 4: Over-Investing in Products Before Validating Demand
Without traffic or audience, Gumroad products won't sell. Validate with email list (100+ subscribers) before investing heavily.

### Mistake 5: Neglecting Analytics
80% of revenue will come from 20% of content. Check Google Analytics weekly. Double down on winners.

### Mistake 6: Single Platform Dependency
By Month 3, have 3+ traffic channels. By Month 6, no single channel should be >50% of revenue.

### Mistake 7: No Systems
By Month 3, manual effort becomes unsustainable. Use Tailwind for pins, Buffer for Twitter, Zapier/Make for email.

### Mistake 8: Revenue Cliff
Passive income compounds slowly. Build 1-month emergency fund by Month 3. Be selective with clients to avoid burnout.
