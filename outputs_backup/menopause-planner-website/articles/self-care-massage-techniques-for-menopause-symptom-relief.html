Common Questions

Can self-massage really help with hot flashes?
Yes, certain self-massage techniques can help regulate blood flow and calm the nervous system, potentially reducing the intensity and frequency of hot flashes. Consistency is key for seeing results.
Which essential oils are best for menopause symptoms?
Lavender, peppermint, clary sage, and geranium are often recommended for their calming and hormone-balancing properties. Always dilute essential oils with a carrier oil before applying to the skin.
How often should I practice self-massage for hot flash relief?
Aim for at least 10-15 minutes daily. You can break it up into shorter sessions throughout the day if that's easier to fit into your schedule.
