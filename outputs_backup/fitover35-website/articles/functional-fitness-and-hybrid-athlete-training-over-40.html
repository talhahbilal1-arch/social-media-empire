Common Questions

Can I really build muscle and run at the same time after 35?
Absolutely! It requires a strategic approach that prioritizes recovery and balances strength and endurance training. Proper nutrition and rest are key.
How often should I run and lift weights?
Aim for 2-3 runs and 2-3 strength training sessions per week, with at least one rest day. Listen to your body and adjust based on your recovery.
What's the best way to prevent injuries with hybrid training?
Prioritize proper warm-ups and cool-downs, incorporate mobility exercises, and gradually increase your training volume. Don't neglect rest and recovery!
