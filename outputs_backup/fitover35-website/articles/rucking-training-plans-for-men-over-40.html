Common Questions

How much weight should I carry when rucking?
Start with 10-20% of your body weight and gradually increase as you get stronger. Listen to your body and don't push yourself too hard, especially when starting.
What kind of shoes are best for rucking?
Trail running shoes or boots with good ankle support and traction are ideal. Avoid flat shoes that can lead to foot fatigue and injuries.
How often should I ruck?
Aim for 2-3 rucking sessions per week, allowing for rest and recovery in between. Vary the distance and intensity to challenge yourself and avoid plateaus.
