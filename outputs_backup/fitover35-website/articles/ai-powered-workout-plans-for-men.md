## How AI Actually Personalizes Your Fitness

Okay, so how does this magic actually happen? Here are a few key ways AI personalizes your workout plan:

*   **Exercise Selection:** It chooses exercises that are appropriate for your fitness level and goals. If you're new to strength training over 35, it won't start you off with Olympic lifts. It might suggest bodyweight exercises like push-ups, squats, and lunges to build a foundation. Smart, right?
