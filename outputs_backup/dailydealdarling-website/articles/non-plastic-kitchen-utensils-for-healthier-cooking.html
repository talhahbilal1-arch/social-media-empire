Common Questions

What types of toxins can leach from kitchen utensils?
Plastic utensils can leach BPA, phthalates, and other endocrine disruptors into food, especially when heated. These chemicals have been linked to various health problems.
How can I tell if my utensils are safe?
Look for utensils made from natural materials like wood or bamboo, or opt for stainless steel or BPA-free silicone. Avoid cheap plastic utensils, especially those with strong odors.
Are all silicone utensils safe?
Not all silicone is created equal. Look for food-grade silicone that is certified BPA-free. Lower quality silicone can still leach chemicals, especially when exposed to high heat.
