## Reddit Tips
- Account should be 30+ days old with 50+ karma before posting links
- Don't post to same subreddit more than 1x per week
- Read subreddit rules before posting (some ban affiliate content)
- Space posts 2-3 hours apart minimum
- Engage in comments — reply to responses genuinely
- r/fitness has strict self-promotion rules — contribute value-first comments for a week before posting
- r/Menopause is supportive but moderators remove anything that looks like marketing
- r/BuyItForLife values longevity data — include time-of-use in every post
- r/Frugal hates affiliate links — make sure the link goes to the article, not directly to Amazon

## Twitter/X Tips
- Best times: 8-10am EST, 12-1pm EST, 5-7pm EST
- Use threads for longer content (break into 2-3 tweets)
- Engage with replies within first hour for algorithm boost
- Schedule using Buffer or Hypefury
- Quote-tweet your own tweets 2-3 days later with added context for extra reach
- Pin your best-performing tweet each week
- Fitness hashtags to rotate: #FitnessOver35, #MensHealth, #HomeGym, #StrengthTraining
- Deals hashtags to rotate: #AmazonFinds, #BudgetLiving, #Frugal, #SmartShopping
- Menopause hashtags to rotate: #Menopause, #WomensHealth, #Perimenopause, #MenopauseRelief

## Pinterest Tips
- Product pins perform best 8pm-11pm EST
- Use 2-5 relevant hashtags
- Pin to most specific board first, then broader boards
- Space pins 15+ minutes apart
- Product pins with price ranges in description get higher click-through rates
- Rich pins should be enabled (verify at developers.pinterest.com)
- Repin top performers to secondary boards 3-5 days later
