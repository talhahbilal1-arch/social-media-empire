## Common Patterns (All 3 Tests)

### Content Pillar Distribution
All 3 tests follow the mandated 40-25-20-15 split:

```
40% — Educational (builds authority, improves algorithm)
25% — Proof/Results (case studies, transformations, testimonials)
20% — Personal/Relatable (behind-the-scenes, real struggles)
15% — Promotional (CTAs, bookings, lead magnets)
```

### 12-Week Calendar Completeness
- **Test 1**: 12 full weeks × 5 posts/week = 60 posts (✓ complete)
- **Test 2**: 12 full weeks × 4 posts/week = 48 posts (✓ complete, LinkedIn format)
- **Test 3**: 12 full weeks × 3 posts/week = 36 videos (✓ complete, TikTok/Reel format)

All calendars include:
- Specific, actionable post titles (not generic fillers)
- Format tags: (C) carousel, (R) reel, (I) image, (T) text-only
- Myth-busting posts marked with * (at least 1 per week)
- Direct CTAs proportional to business model
- Platform-appropriate language

### Three Templates (Every Test)
- **Template A**: Multi-slide carousel with 6-7 specific slides
- **Template B**: Video/reel script with time stamps and exact language
- **Template C**: Caption formula with fill-in-the-blank structure

All templates are immediately usable (not theory — actual words/structure you can adapt).

### Weekly Tracker Table
All 3 include:
- 12-week tracking grid (posts, reach, engagement, conversions)
- Industry-specific KPIs (Fitness: DMs; Agency: download CTR; Photography: qualified lead rate)
- Target breakdown by phase (warmup → momentum → authority)
- Adjustment triggers (when to pivot if performance dips)
