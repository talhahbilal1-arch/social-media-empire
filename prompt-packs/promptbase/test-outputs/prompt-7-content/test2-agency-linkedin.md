## OUTPUT SECTION 1 — CONTENT PILLARS

### Pillar 1: Educational (40% of posts)
**Why this matters**: SaaS founders are drowning in noise and skeptical of quick fixes. Educational content that breaks down demand gen mechanics builds credibility and positions your agency as the strategic partner, not just another vendor.

**Example post topics**:
1. "The 5-part demand gen stack that actually works for $1M-$10M ARR SaaS"
2. "Why your content marketing ROI is invisible (and how to fix it in 30 days)"
3. "The 3 metrics that separate $100K demand gen spends from $1M+ ones"

### Pillar 2: Proof/Results (25% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Founders want to see proof from companies in their revenue band. Case studies showing CAC reduction, pipeline growth, and revenue impact are your strongest conversion tools.

**Example post topics**:
1. "How we cut a SaaS founder's CAC from $6,200 to $2,800 in 90 days (without luck)"
2. "5 companies using the same 3-part system and hitting 30%+ conversion rates"
3. "The demand gen audit that revealed $400K in missed annual revenue"

### Pillar 3: Personal/Relatable (20% of posts)
**Why this matters**: B2B founders are stressed, overworked, and managing 10 conflicting priorities. Posts about scaling pressures, hiring challenges, and real conversations build connection and show you understand their world.

**Example post topics**:
1. "Real talk: Why most SaaS companies can't scale past $5M with their current demand gen setup"
2. "I had 47 conversations with B2B founders last month. Here's what they all got wrong about CAC."
3. "The hidden cost of poor demand gen data (and why founders don't see it until it's too late)"

### Pillar 4: Promotional (15% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Direct CTAs to your audit template convert browsers into conversations. This pillar removes friction and creates a low-risk entry point for founders who aren't ready to commit to full engagement.

**Example post topics**:
1. "Just released: Our free demand gen audit template ($4K value, zero strings attached)"
2. "Spots open for demand gen Q2 consultations (we take 2 per month, first-come basis)"
3. "SaaS founders spend $0 on demand gen audits. Download ours (built from 100+ company reviews)"
