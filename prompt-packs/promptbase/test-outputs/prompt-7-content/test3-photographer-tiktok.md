# 90-Day Content System — Wedding Photography Studio (TikTok)
**Business**: Wedding photography studio
**Audience**: Newly engaged couples aged 25-35 planning their wedding
**Platform**: TikTok
**Goal**: 10 inbound leads/month through organic content

---

## OUTPUT SECTION 1 — CONTENT PILLARS

### Pillar 1: Educational (40% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Engaged couples are overwhelmed by wedding planning decisions. Educational content about photography choices, timing, angles, and what matters builds trust and positions your studio as the expert who cares about getting it right.

**Example post topics**:
1. "The 5 photography moments every couple regrets not having captured at their wedding"
2. "Golden hour myth: Why your best photos aren't taken at sunset (and when to shoot instead)"
3. "The 3 things to do at your engagement shoot that most couples skip"

### Pillar 2: Proof/Results (25% of posts)
**Why this matters**: 25-35 year olds research heavily on social media. Seeing real couples, genuine emotions, and beautiful final galleries builds credibility and social proof. User-generated reviews matter more than your pitch.

**Example post topics**:
1. "Sarah and Mike's first look: The moment they saw each other (and why we never miss this shot)"
2. "Real couple reaction: Emma cried when she saw her wedding photos (here's why this moment mattered)"
3. "5 couples, 5 different vibes, all captured in one season (here's what made each one special)"

### Pillar 3: Personal/Relatable (20% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Wedding planning is stressful. Couples connect with creators who acknowledge the chaos, share real stories, and make them feel understood. Behind-the-scenes and honest takes build relatability.

**Example post topics**:
1. "Real talk: The wedding photo pose that photographers love but couples hate (here's why we changed it)"
2. "Why I almost quit being a wedding photographer (and what changed my mind)"
3. "5 awkward moments from weddings nobody talks about (but happen at every single one)"

### Pillar 4: Promotional (15% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Direct CTAs convert watchers into leads. For TikTok, the CTA is "DM us for pricing guide" and works best when paired with value posts that build urgency and excitement.

**Example post topics**:
1. "Our pricing guide is wild (in a good way). DM 'WEDDING' and we'll send it immediately"
2. "Spots open for 2026 weddings (we book 8-10 per year, some are already gone). DM for availability"
3. "The photo package that changed everything for couples (more details in the DM guide)"
