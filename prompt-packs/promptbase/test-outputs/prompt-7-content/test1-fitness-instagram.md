# 90-Day Content System — Fitness Studio (Instagram)
**Business**: Personal training studio
**Audience**: Men 30-50 wanting to lose their gut and build strength
**Platform**: Instagram
**Goal**: 15 inbound leads/month through organic content

---

## OUTPUT SECTION 1 — CONTENT PILLARS

### Pillar 1: Educational (40% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Men 30-50 are skeptical of fitness advice and want evidence-based strategies. This pillar positions your studio as the knowledgeable authority, not a hype machine.

**Example post topics**:
1. "The 1 macro ratio that cuts belly fat without losing muscle after 40"
2. "Why your daily 10K steps might be blocking your strength gains"
3. "The 4-week protocol that reverses metabolic slowdown in men over 45"

### Pillar 2: Proof/Results (25% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Men in this age range need to see real transformations from people like them. Before/afters build credibility and prove your method works for their demographic.

**Example post topics**:
1. "Marcus: 32 lbs of fat lost, 12 lbs of muscle gained in 16 weeks (zero cardio bunnying)"
2. "How Tom went from "too busy to train" to coaching other dads in 90 days"
3. "The 3 body composition shifts that happened before the scale moved"

### Pillar 3: Personal/Relatable (20% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Men this age are tired and stressed. Relatability builds community and shows you understand their real-world constraints (job stress, recovery needs, family time).

**Example post topics**:
1. "The 5-minute morning routine that saved my energy levels after a brutal week"
2. "Why I stopped counting calories and started building strength instead"
3. "Real talk: How to train hard when you're running on 6 hours of sleep"

### Pillar 4: Promotional (15% of posts)
**Why this matters**: Direct CTAs convert browsers into leads. This pillar focuses on booking consultations and removing friction from the conversion path.

**Example post topics**:
1. "The 3 biggest mistakes I see in men's training (and why our 15-minute assessment catches them)"
2. "Limited: 5 consultation spots left this month (book now, drop $0)"
3. "Your results depend on 2 things: the program AND the coach who understands your goals"
