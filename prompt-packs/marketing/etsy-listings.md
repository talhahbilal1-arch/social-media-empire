## Etsy SEO Notes

**Keyword research shows highest search volume for:**
- "ai fitness prompts" — emerging, low competition
- "chatgpt for coaches" — growing
- "online coach tools" — moderate volume
- "pinterest automation" — niche but high intent

**Title priority:** Put highest-volume keywords in the first 40 characters of the title.
Etsy's algorithm weights the beginning of titles more heavily.

**Tags strategy:** Use full phrases (2-3 words), not single keywords.
"chatgpt fitness prompts" outperforms "chatgpt" + "fitness" + "prompts" separately.

**Photo priority:** The cover image is 70% of your CTR.
Use a clean mockup with readable text and high contrast.
Canva's "Digital Product Mockup" templates work well for this.
