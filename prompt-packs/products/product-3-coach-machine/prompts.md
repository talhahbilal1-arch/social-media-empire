# 50 AI Prompts — Online Coach Client Machine

**How to use:** Copy prompt → fill in [BRACKETS] → paste into ChatGPT or Claude → edit output with your voice.

## STAGE 1: DISCOVERY & QUALIFICATION (Prompts 1–8)

---

**PROMPT 1 — Reply to a Cold DM Inquiry**
```
Someone sent me this DM: "[PASTE THEIR EXACT MESSAGE]"

I'm an online [TYPE: fitness/life/nutrition] coach. My niche: [YOUR NICHE].
My coaching program: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION, 1 sentence].

Write me a reply that:
- Responds naturally and warmly (not like a sales script)
- Shows genuine interest in their situation
- Asks 1-2 questions to understand if they're a good fit
- Does NOT mention price or pitch yet
- Sounds like how I'd text a friend, not a formal business email
- Is under 100 words

Give me 2 versions: casual and slightly more professional.
```

---

**PROMPT 2 — Qualify a Lead Before Booking a Call**
```
I want to send a quick qualification message before booking a discovery call.
A potential client just expressed interest via [DM/email/comment].

Write me 3-4 qualifying questions that will tell me:
- Whether their goal matches what I actually help with
- Their current situation (so the call isn't starting from zero)
- Their timeline and urgency
- If they're a serious buyer or just browsing

Format as a friendly conversational message, not a form.
Under 120 words. Ends with a calendar link invitation.
```

---

**PROMPT 3 — Discovery Call Invitation Message**
```
A prospect has engaged with me for [X] exchanges and seems interested.
Context: [DESCRIBE WHAT THEY'VE SAID / THEIR SITUATION].

Write a natural, low-pressure invitation to a free discovery call that:
- Explains exactly what the call is about (not a sales call — a conversation)
- States the time commitment: "takes about 20 minutes"
- Positions it as me assessing fit, not just pitching
- Has a clear next step (schedule link placeholder)
- Doesn't feel automated or templated

Also give me a subject line if this is being sent as an email.
```

