# 🛍️ Etsy & E-Commerce AI Assistant
## 20 Prompts to Optimize Your Listings, Boost Sales, and Automate Your Shop

> Replace all [brackets] with your specifics. Works for Etsy, Shopify, Amazon Handmade, and any e-commerce platform.

---

## SECTION 1: LISTING OPTIMIZATION (5 Prompts)

### Prompt 1: SEO-Optimized Product Title Generator
```
You are an Etsy SEO expert who has helped shops reach over $100K in annual revenue. Write optimized product titles.

**Product:** [describe your product]
**Category:** [type — e.g., "digital planner," "handmade candle," "print-on-demand t-shirt"]
**Target buyer:** [who buys this]
**Key features:** [material, size, color, use case]
**Platform:** [Etsy / Shopify / Amazon]

Create:
1. 5 SEO-optimized title variations (front-load the most searched keywords)
2. For each: explain which keywords you're targeting and why
3. The character count for each (Etsy max: 140 characters)
4. Long-tail keyword combinations that face less competition
5. Keywords to AVOID (over-saturated or irrelevant)
6. The #1 title recommendation and why it will perform best

Rules for Etsy titles:
- Most important keyword first
- No fluff words that don't help SEO ("beautiful," "amazing")
- Include gift-giving keywords where relevant ("Gift for Mom," "Birthday Gift")
- Separate keyword phrases with commas or pipes
```

### Prompt 2: Product Description That Sells
```
Write a product description that ranks in search AND converts browsers to buyers.

**Product:** [name and description]
**Price:** $[amount]
**Target buyer:** [who is this for]
**Key features:** [5-7 features]
**Materials/Specs:** [relevant details]
**Shipping info:** [timeline, method]
**What makes this different from competitors:** [your USP]
**Platform:** [Etsy / Shopify / Amazon]

Write the full description:
1. Opening paragraph: hook with the BENEFIT, not the feature (2-3 sentences)
2. "Perfect for:" section with 3-5 use cases
3. "What you'll receive:" exact deliverable list
4. "Details & Specs:" measurements, materials, colors
5. "How to use:" instructions (especially for digital products)
6. Shipping & returns info
7. Shop policies reminder
8. Keywords woven in naturally (not keyword-stuffed)

Also provide:
- 13 Etsy tags (if Etsy — these are critical for search)
- Category recommendation
- Attributes to fill in (occasion, color, style, etc.)
```
