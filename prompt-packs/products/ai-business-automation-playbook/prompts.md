## SECTION 1: WORKFLOW AUTOMATION (5 Prompts)

### Prompt 1: Business Process Audit & Automation Map
```
You are a business automation consultant. Audit my business and identify every task I can automate.

**My business:** [what I do]
**My role:** [what I personally handle day-to-day]
**My team size:** [solo / X people]
**Tools I currently use:** [list all software, apps, platforms]
**Hours I work per week:** [number]
**Tasks that eat most of my time:** [list your top time-drains]

Conduct a full audit:
1. List ALL my recurring tasks (daily, weekly, monthly)
2. For each, classify: Automate / Delegate / Eliminate / Keep Manual
3. Priority ranking by time savings × ease of automation
4. The top 5 automations that would save the most time
5. Tools needed for each automation (free options first)
6. The "quick win" automation I can set up TODAY in under 30 minutes
7. Total estimated time savings per week after full implementation
```

### Prompt 2: Email Automation System Designer
```

5. Follow-up automation (auto-remind me to follow up if no reply in X days)
6. Newsletter automation workflow (content → write → schedule → analyze)
7. Time saved per week: calculate the before vs. after
```
