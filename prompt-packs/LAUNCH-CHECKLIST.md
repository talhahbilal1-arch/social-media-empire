## PRE-LAUNCH: What to Prepare Before Going Live

### Step 1: Create PDF Versions of the Products

Each product needs a professionally formatted PDF for delivery.
The content is already written — now format it.

**Option A: Canva (Easiest)**
1. Go to canva.com → Create design → Document
2. Choose an 8.5×11 template or start blank
3. Copy content from the markdown files into text boxes
4. Use a clean, professional font (Inter, Lato, or Poppins)
5. Add your brand colors and a simple cover page
6. Download as PDF
7. Do this for all 3 products and the lead magnet

**Option B: Google Docs (Fast)**
1. Create a Google Doc for each product
2. Paste the markdown content (use Ctrl+Shift+V for plain text paste)
3. Clean up formatting: H1/H2 headers, bullet points, code blocks
4. File → Download as PDF

**For the Google Doc version (included with purchase):**
1. Create the Google Doc
2. Share settings: "Anyone with the link can view"
3. Copy the share link — include this in your Gumroad delivery email

---

### Step 2: Create Product Mockup Images

You need 3-4 product mockup images per Etsy listing.
Cover image is the most important — it's 70% of your CTR.

**Quick method (Canva):**
1. Search Canva for "digital product mockup" or "ebook mockup"
2. Pick a clean template showing a PDF on a laptop or tablet
3. Add your product title, a brief benefit statement, and a clean background
4. Export at 2000×2000px minimum

**For all 3 products, create:**
- 1 cover mockup (product name + main benefit)
- 1 contents preview (shows what's inside)
- 1 "who it's for" graphic (lists the target audience)
- Optional: 1 sample page screenshot

### Step 3: Set Up Gumroad

1. **Create account:** gumroad.com → Sign Up
2. **Set up profile:**
   - Name: [YOUR NAME] or brand name
   - Profile photo: professional headshot (builds trust)
   - Bio: "ISSA CPT | Fitness coach | Building AI tools for the fitness industry"
   - Website: fitover35.com

3. **Create Product 1 (AI Fitness Coach Vault — $27):**
   - New Product → Digital product
   - Upload the PDF file
   - Paste title + description from `marketing/gumroad-listings.md`
   - Set price: $27
   - Add product thumbnail (your mockup image)
   - Checkout settings: collect email address (always)
   - Delivery: Automatic after purchase

4. **Create Product 2 (Pinterest Blueprint — $47):**
   - Upload all 6 markdown files as a ZIP
   - Paste from `marketing/gumroad-listings.md`

5. **Create Product 3 (Coach Machine — $17):**
   - Upload PDF + link to Google Doc in the "thank you" email

6. **Create Lead Magnet (5 Free Prompts — $0):**
   - Set price to $0
   - This goes to your email list for free
   - In Gumroad settings: require email to get the free download

7. **Custom domain (optional):** Connect a custom domain like `products.fitover35.com`
   - Makes links look professional: `products.fitover35.com/l/fitness-vault`

---

### Step 4: Create Etsy Listings

1. **Create Etsy seller account:** etsy.com/sell
2. **Set up your shop:**
   - Shop name: [YOUR BRAND] or something searchable
   - Profile photo + banner
   - About section: your credentials and what you sell

3. **For each product, create a listing:**
   - Navigate to: Shop Manager → Listings → Add a listing
   - Category: Digital Downloads → Guides & How Tos
   - Upload the PDF file
   - Copy title, tags, description from `marketing/etsy-listings.md`
   - Upload 4 mockup images
   - Set price
   - Publish

4. **Etsy policies to set up:**
   - Returns: "Digital downloads are non-refundable" (standard)
   - Shipping: Not applicable (digital)
   - Payment: Connect your bank account

---

### Step 5: Set Up PromptBase Listings (Optional but Recommended)

PromptBase is a discovery channel — low-ticket teaser prompts that build reviews and
drive email signups. New sellers: $4.99 price cap.

1. **Create account:** promptbase.com → Sell prompts
2. **Create 3 teaser listings** (one per product):
   - Product 1 teaser: "Build a Complete 8-Week Training Program for Men Over 35" — $4.99
   - Product 2 teaser: "Pinterest Pin Title Generator (Curiosity-Gap Formula)" — $4.99
   - Product 3 teaser: "Online Coach DM Reply Template — Qualify Without Pitching" — $4.99

3. **In each PromptBase listing description:** Add a line:
   "This is one prompt from the complete [PRODUCT NAME] — [X] prompts available at: [GUMROAD LINK]"

4. **Use PromptBase reviews** as social proof on your Gumroad and Etsy listings once you have them.

---

### Step 6: Set Up ConvertKit Email Sequence

1. **Log in to ConvertKit** (you already have an account with form 5641382)

2. **Create a new automation:**
   - Trigger: Tag added = "prompt-pack-interested" OR clicked link in email
   - Action: Start sequence "Prompt Pack Launch"

3. **Create the sequence:** Use all 7 emails from `marketing/email-sequence.md`
   - Set the delays between each email exactly as listed
   - Add each email's A/B subject line options

4. **Create a one-time broadcast** to your existing list:
   - Use Email 1 from the sequence as the first broadcast
   - Send to your entire fitover35.com list
   - This is your cold launch — no warm-up needed, they already know you

5. **Set up lead magnet delivery:**
   - New form in ConvertKit just for the free 5 prompts
   - When someone subscribes: tag as "prompt-pack-interested" → triggers the 7-email sequence
   - Deliver the PDF automatically via the confirmation email

## First 10 Sales Strategy

The hardest part is the first 10 sales. Here's the fastest path:

**1. Personal outreach to your existing network (Days 1-3)**
- DM 10-15 fitness coaches or men over 35 in your network personally
- Don't mass-message — personalize each one
- "Hey [NAME] — I made something that you specifically might find useful because [REASON]. It's $27. Want me to send you the link?"
- First 5 sales often come from personal outreach, not discovery

**2. Post in relevant communities (Week 1)**
- Reddit: r/personaltraining, r/fitness35plus, r/ftm35 — share the free lead magnet, not the paid product
- Facebook Groups for fitness coaches
- Rule: share value first (the free 5 prompts), mention the paid version second

**3. Your existing Pinterest audience**
- Your Pinterest accounts already get traffic
- Pin the product pins into your regular rotation
- The algorithm already serves your content to relevant people

**4. Email list (you have this)**
- You already have fitover35.com subscribers
- This is your warmest audience — they already trust you
- The 7-email sequence handles this

## Pricing Strategy Post-Launch

**First 30 days:** Hold prices at launch prices ($27 / $47 / $17)
**After first 5 reviews on each product:** Raise Product 1 to $37, keep others same
**After first 10 reviews:** Consider adding a $67 bundle (all 3 products)
**Never:** Drop prices publicly (it signals low quality). Use private coupon codes for personal outreach instead.
