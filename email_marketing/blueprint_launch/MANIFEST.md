# Blueprint Launch Sequence

## Product
The Fit Over 35 Blueprint - $97 one-time
12-week strength, nutrition, and recovery plan for men 35+
Landing page: https://fitover35.com/products/blueprint/

## Sequence Overview
A 5-email broadcast sequence sent over 6 days to existing fitover35
subscribers. Each email is plain-text friendly, no fake urgency, no
manipulative copy. The Inner Circle ($27/mo) is mentioned as a soft P.S.
on the final two emails only.

| Day | Email | Subject (A) | Tone |
|-----|-------|-------------|------|
| 0 | 01-announcement | The 12-week plan for men over 35 is finally here | Warm announcement, soft pitch |
| 2 | 02-value-email | Why every program you've tried after 35 failed | Free education, soft CTA |
| 4 | 03-case-study | A 42yo, 215lb, desk-job story | Composite case study, stronger CTA |
| 5 | 04-objection-handling | "I don't have time for 5-6 workouts per week" (you don't need to) | Time objection, strong CTA |
| 6 | 05-close | Last email about the Blueprint (for now) | Calm close, guarantee, reply invite |

## Target Audience
- Kit form ID: 8946984 (fitover35)
- From name: Fit Over 35
- From email: hello@fitover35.com
- Reply-to: hello@fitover35.com

## Segment Filters
Send to subscribers who:
- Are tagged `brand:fitness` OR signed up via form 8946984
- Are NOT tagged `purchased:blueprint`
- Have engaged with at least one email in the last 90 days

## KPIs
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Open rate | > 35% |
| Click-through rate | > 8% |
| Unsubscribe rate | < 0.5% per email |
| Conversion (CTR -> purchase) | > 2% |
| Reply rate (final email) | > 1% |

## A/B Testing
Each email has Subject A and Subject B. Send Subject A to 50% of the
list, Subject B to the other 50%. Pick the winner for the next launch.

## Notes
- All CTAs link to https://fitover35.com/products/blueprint/
- The case study in email 03 is a fictional composite, clearly labeled
  as such inside the email body. No fabricated testimonials.
- Inner Circle ($27/mo) appears only in P.S. of emails 04 and 05.
- Send schedule: 09:00 PT on each scheduled day.
