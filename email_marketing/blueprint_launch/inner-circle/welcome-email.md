## Body

Hey,

Thanks for joining the Inner Circle. A short welcome so you know
exactly what is coming and when.

**What you will receive**

- **Mondays** - one training note, usually 200-400 words. A
  programming idea, a lift cue, or an adjustment for the week.
- **Thursdays** - one applied-knowledge email, usually 400-700 words.
  Nutrition, recovery, injury-management, or a supplement breakdown.
- **First Monday of each month** - one long-form essay, 1500-2500
  words. Deeper dives on the topics that do not fit in a short email.

That is three to five emails per month. No more. I want this to be
something you actually open, not a folder you mute.

**Members-only archive**

Every essay and training note ever sent is in the archive. The link
is at the bottom of every email. You can read backwards as far as you
want.

**Billing**

$27/month, billed monthly. Cancel any time from your account page or
by replying to any email I send. The first month has a 7-day refund
window if it is not what you expected.

**The first email arrives Monday**

Your first training note lands this coming Monday at 7am Pacific. It
is a short one on warm-up sequencing - the thing most over-35 lifters
skip and then pay for in the second set.

If you are still working through the 12-week Blueprint, the Inner
Circle content runs in parallel. Nothing here will conflict with the
program. The training notes are designed to plug into your current
phase.

If you have a specific question you want me to cover in a future
email, just hit reply. I read everything to this address. No promise
I get to every topic, but the inbox shapes what I write.

That is it. Thanks for being here.

Stay strong,

The Fit Over 35 Team

P.S. The archive link will be active in your account by tomorrow
morning. If you do not see it by then, reply and I will sort it.
