# Inner Circle - $27/mo Subscription

## Product
The Fit Over 35 Inner Circle - $27/month recurring subscription, billed
monthly via Kit's paid newsletter feature. Cancel any time from the
account page, no friction.

## Positioning
The Inner Circle is **continued programming and weekly applied content**
for men 35+ who finished (or will finish) the 12-week Blueprint and want
ongoing structure without buying a new program every quarter.

It is **not**:

- A coaching service
- A community/forum (we are not building a Discord)
- A content firehose with daily emails
- An "exclusive" anything that uses scarcity language

It **is**:

- One programming-focused email each Monday (training note)
- One applied-knowledge email each Thursday (nutrition, recovery,
  injury-management, or supplement breakdown)
- One long-form essay each month (1500-2500 words, deep dive on one
  topic)
- A members-only archive of every essay and training note ever sent

That is the whole offer. Three to five emails per month plus the
archive. Calm cadence, real value, easy cancel.

## Pricing
- $27/month, billed monthly
- No annual upsell at launch (we may revisit later)
- 7-day refund window on the first month if it is not what they
  expected
- Kit handles billing through their paid newsletter feature

## Target Subscriber
- Bought the Blueprint and finished or is in the middle of it
- Wants continued programming without the cost of 1-on-1 coaching
- Reads long-form, prefers email over apps
- Treats $27/mo as a coffee-budget line item, not a major purchase

## KPIs
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Conversion from Blueprint buyers | > 8% |
| Month-2 retention | > 80% |
| Month-6 retention | > 60% |
| Cancellation reply rate | > 25% |
| Average tenure | > 4 months |

## Files
- welcome-email.md - day 0 email new subscribers receive
- week-1-to-4-cadence.md - what they receive weekly for the first month
- cancellation-prevention.md - email that fires on cancellation request

## Tone Rules
- No emojis
- No "exclusive," "VIP," or "elite" language
- No urgency, no FOMO, no "wait list closing"
- Cancellation flow is genuinely easy and never guilt-trips
- All claims about results are realistic and bracketed
