## Sequence Settings

### Timing
- Email 1: Immediately
- Email 2: +2 days
- Email 3: +4 days
- Email 4: +6 days
- Email 5: +8 days
- Email 6: +11 days
- Email 7: +14 days

### Tags to Add
- `welcome-sequence-started`
- `lead-magnet-{name}`
- After completion: `welcome-sequence-completed`

### Automation Triggers
- After Email 7: Move to weekly newsletter
- If opens < 2 emails: Add to re-engagement sequence
- If clicks link: Add `engaged-subscriber` tag
