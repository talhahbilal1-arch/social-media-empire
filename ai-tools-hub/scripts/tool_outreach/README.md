## Outreach Tips

**Timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am in recipient's timezone. Avoid Mondays and Fridays.

**Follow-up cadence:** Send initial email → wait 5 business days → one follow-up only. Do not send a third.

**Personalize the subject line:** Tools get dozens of cold pitches. Mentioning their tool name in the subject (not just "partnership opportunity") doubles open rates.

**Track responses:** Log each outreach in `config/outreach-log.json` with date, tool, tier pitched, and outcome.

**Goal conversion rate:** 5–10% for upgraded listings is realistic. With 20 targets, expect 1–2 paid conversions from a round of outreach.
